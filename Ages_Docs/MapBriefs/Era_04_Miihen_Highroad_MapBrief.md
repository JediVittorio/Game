# Map Brief: Mi'ihen Highroad (Map 032)
**Era:** 4 — Conditional Timeline
**Theme:** Scenic coastal cliffs, pilgrimage route.
**Visual Style:** FFX-style linear cinematic path. Uses a high-detail coastal cliff parallax to create depth.

## Spatial Layout
- **Path:** Extremely linear, stretching from West (Map 031 transition) to East. The path is narrow, hugging the cliffside.
- **Perspective:** Side-on cinematic view to emphasize the height of the cliffs and the vastness of the ocean parallax.

## Key Locations
1. **The Highroad Entrance:** Transition point from Besaid-style environments.
2. **The Cliff Overlook:** A small protrusion in the path where the "Save Sphere" is located.
3. **The Chocobo Post:** A small wooden stall where the rental NPC stands.

## NPC Placement
- **Highroad Traveler:** Walking slowly towards the East. Dialogue: "The pilgrimage is long, but the view... the view never changes."
- **Chocobo Rent-a-Bird:** NPC with a yellow bird sprite. Dialogue: "Renting this bird will make the linear path feel 50% faster!"
- **Glitched Guard:** Standing near the exit. Dialogue: "The path ahead is restricted to those who carry the weight of their data."

## Interaction: Save Sphere
- **Sprite:** Glowing blue sphere.
- **Dialogue:** "This sphere records your data and your sins. Mostly your data."
- **Effect:** Full HP/MP restore + Save Menu.

## Interaction: Chocobo Rental
- **Cost:** 100 Gil.
- **Effect:** Grants **State ID 62: Mounted**.
- **State Logic:** Increases Move Speed by 100%. Removed on map transfer or interaction with "Dismount" signs.

## Encounter Zones
- **Highroad Path:** Mi'ihen Fang (ID 93), Dual Horn (ID 94).
