# Map Brief: Besaid Beach (Map 031)
**Era:** 4 — Conditional Timeline
**Theme:** Tropical coastal paradise.
**Visual Style:** FFX-style pre-rendered background path. High azure water, white sand, lush greenery.

## Spatial Layout
- **Path:** A linear "S" curve starting from the water's edge (bottom-left) and leading to the jungle entrance (top-right).
- **Inlets:** Two small alcoves in the rock walls containing "Pre-rendered" treasure chests (static events).
- **Z-Axis:** A small wooden pier jutting into the water.

## Key Locations
1. **The Shoreline:** Where the player spawns. Clear, animated water tiles.
2. **The Overlook:** A slightly elevated rocky ledge (Region 5) looking over the ocean.
3. **The Jungle Gate:** The transition point to the next map.

## NPC Placement
- **Lulu-Equiv:** Standing on the overlook, gazing at the sea.
- **Wakka-Equiv:** Near the pier, practicing with a blitzball (animation loop).
- **Villager (Child):** Running along the shoreline.

## Gimmick: "The Sending"
- Interacting with certain "Glitched Pyreflies" (Event) triggers a short CTB-style practice battle with a holographic training dummy.

## Encounter Zones
- **Zone 1 (Shore):** Dingo-Equiv (ID 91).
- **Zone 2 (Jungle Entrance):** Condor-Equiv (ID 92).
