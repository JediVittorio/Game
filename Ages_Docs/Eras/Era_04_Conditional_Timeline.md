# Era 4: Conditional Timeline
**Baseline Title:** Final Fantasy X (2001)
**STATUS:** IN PROGRESS

## Narrative Premise
The world is a sequence of moments, each a drop in the ocean of the Spiral. The party arrives on a sun-drenched beach, the air thick with the scent of salt and the weight of a pilgrimage that never ends. Here, time isn't a river, but a series of choices that can be seen before they are made.

The protagonist, a displaced star from a city that shouldn't exist, must navigate a world gripped by the "Spiral of Stasis"—a cycle of destruction that can only be delayed, never truly broken.

## Setting Details
- **Primary Locations:** Besaid Beach, Port Kilika, Luca Stadium.
- **Visual Style:** Pre-rendered background aesthetics, high-detail coastal environments, azure saturation.
- **Tone Keywords:** Melancholic, bittersweet, introspective.

## Party Characters
### Tidus-Archetype
- **Role:** Blitz-Star (High Agility, CTB specialist)
- **Personality:** Boisterous on the surface, but deeply confused and mourning a lost home.
- **Background:** A star athlete from a futuristic city that was destroyed by the "Spiral" 1,000 years ago—or so he's told.
- **Motivation:** To find a way home, only to realize "home" is a memory.

## Major NPCs
- **The High Summoner:** A recurring archetypal figure seeking to perform the "Sending" of the glitched eras.
- **The Blitzball Captain:** A rugged mentor figure obsessed with a game that distracts from the apocalypse.

## Key Story Beats
1. **The Shore:** Tidus wakes up on Map 031, confused by the bright sunlight and the lack of neon.
2. **The Ritual:** Witnessing a "Sending" in Kilika-Equiv, realizing the eras are being deleted.
3. **The Game:** A mandatory Blitzball match where the CTB system is fully tested.
4. **The Sending of the Gate:** Beating the Era Boss to open the way to Era 5.

## Map Brief Summary
Map 031 is a linear coastal path with hidden inlets. NPCs are villagers who speak of "The Spiral" and the "Sin of the Glitch."
