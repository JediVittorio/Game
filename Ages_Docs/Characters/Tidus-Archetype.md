# Tidus-Archetype
- **Actor ID:** 31
- **Class:** Blitz-Star
- **Era:** 4
- **Weapon:** Brotherhood-Equiv (Longsword)
- **Role:** High-Agility Striker. Specializes in delaying enemy turns and accelerating allies via CTB manipulation.

## Personality
Confused, loud, and prone to internal monologues. He is the lens through which the player sees the "weirdness" of the world of Era 4.

## Background
The star player of the Zanarkand-Equiv Blitzball team. He was sucked into a portal by a giant glitch-whale and spat out in a world that treats him like a ghost.

## Motivation
"This is my story. And it'll be over before I even know it."
