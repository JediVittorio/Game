# Stratum 3: The Moonscorched Menagerie — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Interaction Objects (Strategic Tools)

### The Screaming Bell (Pushable)
*   **Description:** A massive, rusted iron bell mounted on a wooden frame.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It was used to summon the citizens to prayer. Now its chime only summons the hungry things in the dark. It’s loud, it’s heavy, and it’s very effective at drawing attention."
*   **Effect:** Used to lure the **Sentinel of Regret**. Emits a loud sound wave that travels 5 tiles.

### The Sentimental Item (e.g., Faded Photograph)
*   **Description:** A small object from the protagonist's past.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The only thing that still has color. Giving it away feels like losing a limb, but it’s the only language the Sentinel understands."
*   **Effect:** Can be "Traded" to the **Sentinel** to open the path peacefully.

### The Ledger’s Coin
*   **Description:** A massive, gold coin with a screaming face on both sides.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Obtained by striking the Ledger of Agony. It is cursed, heavy, and smells like a corporate merger. Carrying it makes your movements sluggish."
*   **Effect:** Distracts the Ledger. Inflicts +20 **Guilt** and reduces movement speed by 25%.

## 2. Meta-Knowledge Objects (Variables)

### The Bloody Solution (Var 602)
*   **Description:** The geometric pattern required to solve the Floor 11 Taskmaster puzzle.
*   **Flavor Text:** "You saw it in the flash of your own death. Three up, two left, one down. It’s etched into the back of your retinas."
*   **Effect:** Allows the player to solve the Floor 11 puzzle on subsequent loops.

## 3. Consumables (Surgical Sustenance)

### Antiseptic Swab
*   **Description:** A piece of cotton soaked in a liquid that smells like industrial bleach.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Use it to clean the 'Corrupted Flesh' from your Rusted Blade. It burns the skin, but it sharpens the mind."
*   **Effect:** Restores 10 MP. Increases Accuracy for 3 turns.

### Rusted Coin (Bait)
*   **Description:** A standard gold coin from Stage 1, now covered in rust and grit.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Throw it into an 'Inventory Pit' to temporarily disable the trap. It’s a small price to pay for your sanity."
*   **Effect:** Disables an **Inventory Pit** for 5 seconds.
