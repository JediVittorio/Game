# Stratum 3: The Moonscorched Menagerie — NPC Profiles
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## The Moonscorched Residents (Satirical Inversions)

### 1. The Taskmaster of the Void (Originally: The Quest Giver)
*   **Visuals:** A multi-limbed entity bound by glowing red strings. Each limb is frantically rearranging debris into geometric patterns. It has a massive, unblinking smile and weeping eyes.
*   **Personality:** Pathologically obsessed with "Purpose." He cannot exist without a task.
*   **Narrative Role:** He forces the player into a complex, meaningless puzzle. He represents the "Endless Fetch Quest" of Stage 1.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Weeping) The pattern must be complete! Do you have the three widgets? Do you have the two gears? Why are you not organizing the void?!"

### 2. The Sentinel of Regret (Originally: The Town Guard)
*   **Visuals:** A hulking, immobile mass of fused shields and armor plating. It has no face, only a single "Regret Aura" that pulses blue.
*   **Personality:** Paralyzed by a past failure. He believes that by standing still, he can prevent anything bad from ever happening again.
*   **Narrative Role:** An impenetrable wall. He represents the "Passive Obstacle" in Stage 1.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Hollow Echo) No one passes. If no one passes, no one can fail. Stay. Be safe. Be still. Like me."

### 3. The Devouring Ego (Originally: The Overpowered Mentor)
*   **Visuals:** A floating, withered core surrounded by a halo of shattered mirrors. The mirrors project images of the mentor figure in his prime.
*   **Personality:** A narcissist who feeds on attention. He is terrified of being irrelevant.
*   **Narrative Role:** A combat mirror. He represents the "God-Mode NPC" that solved the player's problems in Stage 1.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Screaming) Look at me! Look at what I’ve achieved! Your attacks are just a tribute to my greatness! Do not stop! I NEED your hate!"

### 4. The Ledger of Agony (Originally: The Merchant)
*   **Visuals:** A sprawling, immobile mass of rusted coins and human skin. It has dozens of mouths, each one counting and recounting its own rotting flesh.
*   **Personality:** A hoarder driven by a fear of starvation.
*   **Narrative Role:** An inventory drain. He represents the "Economy Sink" of Stage 1.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Wet Clinking) Ten fingers... nine toes... fifty-seven regrets... What do you have for me, meat-creature? Give it all. The inventory must be full. The ledger must balance."

## The Shadow Architect

### 5. The Architect of Atrophy (Presence)
*   **Visuals:** A formless, black cloud that occupies the corner of the Awakening Room (Phase 2). It has no physical features, but it "vibrates" with the sound of a failing hard drive.
*   **Narrative Role:** A passive hazard. It represents the "Rot" of the simulation.
*   **Aura:** Drains **Mind** resource by 1 per second.
