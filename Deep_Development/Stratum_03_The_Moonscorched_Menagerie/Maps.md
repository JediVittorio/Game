# Stratum 3: The Moonscorched Menagerie — Detailed Map Specifications
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## Overview: The Surgical Town
Stratum 3 is a rusted, metallic, and "surgical" interpretation of the lively towns from Stage 1. The architecture is composed of rusted pipes, surgical tables, and flickering monitors. The air smells like antiseptic and dry rot. The ground is made of "Flesh-Tile" that reacts with a wet squelch when stepped on.

---

## Floor 11: The Taskmaster of the Void
*   **Atmosphere:** Frantic and clinical. The sound of a thousand ticking clocks fills the room.
*   **Layout:** A large open space filled with "Pointless Debris"—shattered furniture and broken gears.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Taskmaster’s Grid:** A UI-based push puzzle area where the player must arrange blocks into a specific pattern.
*   **Gimmick:** The pattern is only visible on the death screen. The player **must** die once to see the solution written in blood on the "Return" vortex. The time limit is 10 seconds.

---

## Floor 12: The Sentinel of Regret
*   **Atmosphere:** Oppressive and immobile. The air feels heavy.
*   **Layout:** A wide corridor blocked by the **Sentinel of Regret** NPC.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Screaming Bell:** A push-block that emits a loud chime, used for stealth misdirection.
*   **Gimmick:** The Sentinel is invincible and reflects all damage as a permanent **Guilt** stat increase. The player must use the Screaming Bell to lure it away from the door, or offer a "Sentimental Item" (like the Faded Photograph) to ask it to step aside.

---

## Floor 13: The Devouring Ego
*   **Atmosphere:** Narcissistic and fragile. The walls are covered in shattered mirrors.
*   **Layout:** A circular room with the **Devouring Ego** NPC in the center.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Reflection Halo:** An aura around the NPC that copies the player's verbs.
*   **Gimmick:** The boss cannot be out-damaged. The player must **refuse to swing**. By standing still and accepting hits, the entity starves of validation and eventually dissolves.

---

## Floor 14: The Ledger of Agony
*   **Atmosphere:** Greedy and rotting. The sound of coins clinking is muffled by the sound of wet skin.
*   **Layout:** A room filled with "Inventory Pits"—traps that drain items instead of HP.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Moonscorched Merchant:** A recognizable version of the Stage 1 merchant, now transformed.
*   **Gimmick:** The player must willingly drop valuable items to distract the Ledger. Striking the merchant yields a "Gold Coin" that distracts the Ledger but inflicts maximum **Guilt**. The "pure" solution is to talk to the merchant and learn the exit's location.

---

## Floor 15: Checkpoint 3 — The Awakening Room (Phase 2: Fragmentation)
*   **Atmosphere:** The room geometry is fracturing. Walls are missing, revealing a sheer drop into a starless void. Mirrors show monstrous silhouettes.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Bed:** Health restore is capped at 80%. UI elements shake if the player stays for more than 30 seconds.
    *   **The Architect of Atrophy (Presence):** A formless dark shape in the corner that saps **Mind** resource by 1 per second.
*   **Gimmick:** This is the first "Unsafe" safe room. The player must rest fast or rest never.
