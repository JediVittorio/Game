# Stratum 3: The Moonscorched Menagerie — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. The Moonscorched Entities

### The Taskmaster of the Void (NPC)
*   **Aesthetic:** A nightmare of red strings and many arms. The sprite "twitches" every few frames.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Rearrange:** His limbs move in a blur, swapping debris tiles on the grid.
    *   **The Weep:** A fountain of black ink sprays from his eyes when the player enters his line of sight.

### The Sentinel of Regret (NPC)
*   **Aesthetic:** A wall of metal. You can see the faces of Stage 1 guards pressed into the shields like relief sculptures.
*   **Animations:** It does not walk. When it "moves" (pushed by the player), it lets out a sound like a ship’s hull grinding against an iceberg.

### The Devouring Ego (NPC)
*   **Aesthetic:** A core of gray dust surrounded by dozens of floating mirror fragments. Each fragment shows a different "Heroic Pose."
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Mimicry:** The mirror fragments rotate to face the player, showing a reflection of the player's own actions.
    *   **The Dissolve:** When defeated (by inaction), the mirrors shatter and the core turns to white static.

### The Ledger of Agony (NPC)
*   **Aesthetic:** A mound of skin and metal. Coins are embedded in its flesh like scales.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Count:** Dozens of mouths open and close in sequence, with a sound like a slot machine.
    *   **The Drain:** A vacuum-like effect that pulls items from the player's sprite toward the entity.

## 2. Awakening Room (Phase 2: Fragmentation)

### Fractured Geometry (Environment)
*   **Description:** The floor and walls have gaps that show the scrolling "Starless Void" background.
*   **Animations:** The edges of the gaps "flicker" with white pixels. The remaining walls "sway" as if the room is floating on water.

### The Architect of Atrophy (Entity)
*   **Aesthetic:** A formless, roiling mass of dark smoke.
*   **Animations:** It does not move, but its "Aura" (visible gray waves) pulses in rhythm with the protagonist's heartbeat.

## 3. Environment Visuals

### Flesh-Tile (Floor 11-14)
*   **Description:** A ground texture that looks like muscle and skin.
*   **Animations:** When stepped on, the tile "depresses" and a wet squelching sound plays. Small "veins" pulse with blue light.

### The Screaming Bell (Object)
*   **Description:** A large, cracked bell.
*   **Animations:** When pushed, the bell swings and emits a visible circle of "Sound Waves" (white outlines) that alerts nearby entities.

### Inventory Pits (Traps)
*   **Description:** Holes in the floor that look like gaping mouths.
*   **Animations:** When the player walks over them, a "Gulp" sound plays and a random item icon is seen falling into the hole.
