# Stratum 5: The Court_of_the_Forgotten — NPC Profiles
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## The Jurors of Repression

### 1. The Gatekeeper (The Frame-Warden)
*   **Visuals:** A towering, stone-like figure wearing a barrister’s wig made of actual white snakes. He holds a massive brass key and a stopwatch that ticks backwards.
*   **Personality:** Cold, precise, and literal. He does not accept arguments; he only accepts "Synchronized Evidence."
*   **Narrative Role:** Guards the entrance to the courtroom. He forces the player to understand that time and timing are the only laws that matter.
*   **Dialogue:** "You are out of sync with the court, User. Your entry frame was 4059; the law requires 4060. You are... unauthorized. Please submit to deletion."

### 2. The Jester (The Mockery)
*   **Visuals:** A spindly figure in a court-jester outfit that is half-gold, half-rotting flesh. He carries a small, squeaky mallet that can move 10-ton blocks.
*   **Personality:** Manic and cruel. He views the player’s attempt to find "Justification" as the ultimate joke.
*   **Narrative Role:** The disruptor of Floor 22. He forces the player to choose between patience and violence.
*   **Dialogue:** "Oh, look at you! Pushing your little blocks! Trying to make sense of it all! Let me help you with that... oops! Back to the start! Hahaha!"

### 3. The Twin Witnesses (The Paradox)
*   **Visuals:** Two identical girls in white dresses, sitting on a marble bench. One has a black ribbon; the other has a white one.
*   **Personality:** One is catatonically silent; the other is a pathological liar.
*   **Narrative Role:** The gatekeepers of Floor 23. They represent the protagonist's inability to distinguish between the truth of their trauma and the lies of their satire.
*   **Dialogue:** "One of us is a memory. One of us is a hope. If you kill the hope, the memory will scream the way out. If you kill the memory... there is no out."

### 4. The Executioner (The Chronicler)
*   **Visuals:** A massive, hooded figure with no visible face. His "axe" is a giant, sharpened scroll.
*   **Personality:** Solemn and clerical. He doesn't hate the player; he is simply recording the "Final Sentence."
*   **Narrative Role:** The judge of Floor 24. He reads the **DEATH_COUNT** and lists the names of every NPC the player has "bridged" or "sacrificed."

## The Sovereign

### 5. Sovereign of Cognitive Dissonance (The Boss)
*   **Visuals:** A swirling mass of Stage 1 assets—a Blitzball, a spiky hair-wig, a paper hammer—all held together by glowing red static. In the center is a single, weeping eye.
*   **Personality:** Contradictory. It speaks in two voices simultaneously: one cheerful and comedic, the other guttural and terrified.
*   **Narrative Role:** The final test of "Persuasion." It represents the conflict between the player's nostalgia and the protagonist's reality.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Screaming) IT WAS ALL A GAME! (Cheerfully) Let’s go on an adventure! (Screaming) WE ARE DYING IN THE DARK! (Cheerfully) Don't forget to save your progress!"

## The Whispering Apparitions

### 6. The Ghosts of Scabs
*   **Description:** Translucent, blue-tinted sprites of every Stage 1 NPC the player has killed or used in Stage 2.
*   **Aura:** They surround the Floor 25 bed, preventing "Rest" until the player speaks to each one and accepts their "Verbatim Truth."
