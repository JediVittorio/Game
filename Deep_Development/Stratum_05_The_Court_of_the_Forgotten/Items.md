# Stratum 5: The Court of the Forgotten — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Legalistic Tools (Interaction Gear)

### Justification Blocks (Pushable)
*   **Description:** Massive slabs of white marble with justifications carved into them: "I HAD NO CHOICE," "THEY WANTED THIS," "IT WAS FOR THE BEST."
*   **Flavor Text:** "They are much heavier than they look. The more reasonable the justification, the harder it is to move. Pushing them feels like lying to a mirror."
*   **Effect:** Used on Floor 22 to bridge the gap.

### The Brass Key of Frame
*   **Description:** A complex key with a clock face built into the handle.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It only turns when the universe is at a standstill. It doesn't unlock a door; it unlocks a moment."
*   **Effect:** Found by defeating the Gatekeeper’s Evasion Puzzle. Unlocks the Floor 21 door.

### The Jammed Blade (Sword Upgrade)
*   **Description:** The Rusted Blade, now with a piece of the Guillotine’s track wedged into its notch.
*   **Flavor Text:** "You used your own death to break the machine. The blade is jagged and ugly now, but it carries the weight of a survived sentence."
*   **Effect:** Increases the **Swing** verb's damage and provides immunity to "Capture" events.

## 2. Evidence (Key Items)

### The Executioner’s List
*   **Description:** A long scroll that unrolls infinitely. It contains every death and every kill the player has recorded.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Reading it is like looking at a ledger of your own soul's erosion. You are currently at [Var 701] deaths. The court is unimpressed."
*   **Effect:** Displays current Stage 2 statistics when inspected.

### The White Ribbon (Paradox Item)
*   **Description:** A stained silk ribbon obtained from the "Truthful" Twin.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It smells like ozone and a child's funeral. Holding it makes your directional inputs flip randomly for 5 seconds."
*   **Effect:** Used to weaken the Sovereign of Cognitive Dissonance.

---

## 3. Consumables (Juridical Sustenance)

### Sealing Wax
*   **Description:** A stick of red wax.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Use it to 'Seal' your lips. It prevents the player from using the **Speak** verb for 3 turns, but protects against 'Contradiction' damage."
*   **Effect:** Grants immunity to Sovereign's "Contradiction" status.

### Parchment Scraps
*   **Description:** Bits of paper containing legal arguments.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Eating them makes your stomach feel full of dust. It restores a small amount of HP but makes you 'Silent.'"
*   **Effect:** Restores 50 HP. Inflicts "Silence" (Cannot use Speak).

## 4. Meta-Knowledge Objects (Variables)

### The Frame Secret (Var 603)
*   **Description:** The mathematical pattern behind the Gatekeeper’s password.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Entry Frame + Death Count = Solution. It’s so simple it’s offensive. Now that you know, the Gatekeeper can never truly stop you again."
*   **Effect:** Automatically solves the Floor 21 keypad on subsequent loops.
