# Stratum 5: The Court of the Forgotten — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. The Jurors & Judges

### The Gatekeeper (NPC)
*   **Aesthetic:** A stone giant wearing a judge’s robe. His "Wig" is a writhing mass of white vipers. He holds a brass key as a staff.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Ticking:** A large stopwatch on his chest pulses with a red light.
    *   **The Verdict:** When he attacks, he slams his key-staff down, and the screen "splits" for a frame.

### The Jester (NPC)
*   **Aesthetic:** A spindly, skeletal figure in a dual-colored outfit (Gold and Rotting Flesh). He wears a mask that has a laughing face on one side and a weeping face on the other.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Mocking Dance:** He skips around the blocks, occasionally sticking his tongue out at the player.
    *   **The Mallet Swing:** He hits a 10-ton block with a tiny mallet, which emits a "Squeak" sound and a comic-book style "BONK!" particle.

### The Twin Witnesses (NPCs)
*   **Aesthetic:** Pale, grayscale girls in white dresses. One carries a black ribbon; the other a white one. Their eyes are just black voids.
*   **Animations:** They sit perfectly still, but their ribbons flutter as if in a strong wind. When one is killed, she dissolves into a pile of white petals.

### The Executioner (NPC)
*   **Aesthetic:** A massive figure in a black executioner’s hood. He has no arms; his "Axe" is a giant scroll that floats behind him.
*   **Animations:** The scroll "unrolls" across the floor to track the player. When it strikes, it makes the sound of heavy paper tearing.

## 2. The Sovereign

### Sovereign of Cognitive Dissonance (Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A roiling vortex of Stage 1 memory fragments. You can see Tadpole’s Blitzball, Krono’s spiky wig, and Maurice’s hammer spinning in the red static. In the center is a human-like weeping eye.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Control Shuffle:** The screen flashes white, and the player’s sprite briefly transforms into a random Stage 1 character.
    *   **The Contradiction:** The Boss fires a beam that, if it hits, causes the "OK" and "CANCEL" buttons to swap functions for 10 seconds.

---

## 3. Environment Visuals

### Unbalanced Scales (Floor 21-25)
*   **Description:** Massive golden scales that are built into the walls or floor.
*   **Animations:** They tip violently when the player moves nearby. One tray is filled with Stage 1 "Comedy" assets; the other is filled with Stage 2 "Grief" ashes.

### Evidence Floor (Floor 21-25)
*   **Description:** A ground texture covered in polaroid photos, children’s toys, and legal documents.
*   **Animations:** When the player walks over the "Evidence," the photos "flicker" to show a clear, full-color image of the trauma figure for 0.1 seconds.

### The Guillotine (Floor 24)
*   **Description:** A towering wooden structure. The blade is a giant, sharpened "Zoning Permit."
*   **Animations:** The blade vibrates as it descends. When jammed by the sword, it emits a shower of metallic sparks and black "ERROR" particles.
