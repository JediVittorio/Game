# Stratum 7: The Depths of Apathy — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. The Final Tools (Closure Gear)

### The SWING Block (Pushable)
*   **Description:** A massive, glowing white block with the word "SWING" carved into its side. It radiates a faint, aggressive energy.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The sum of all your violent solutions. Every strike, every kill, and every parry you’ve ever performed is contained within this block. To let it go is to admit that you can't fight your way out of this."
*   **Effect:** Pushed into the furnace on Floor 34 to open the final door.

### The Ashes of Justice
*   **Description:** A pile of gray dust in your inventory where the Rusted Blade used to be.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It’s gone. The blade, the notch, and the sound of the metal. You feel lighter, but more vulnerable than you’ve ever been. There is no one left to strike."
*   **Effect:** Replaces the **Swing** verb in the menu.

## 2. Meta-Knowledge Objects (Variables)

### The Truth (Var 605)
*   **Description:** The realization that the Sovereign has never attacked.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Every death you experienced in the void was your own action. You pushed the illusion. You stepped on the trap. You made the noise. The Sovereign only watched."
*   **Effect:** Unlocks the "Inaction" victory condition on Floor 35.

### The Inaction Timer (Var 708)
*   **Description:** A hidden variable that tracks the player's stillness.
*   **Effect:** Every frame without input increases this variable. At 10,800 (180 seconds), the game ends.
