# Stratum 7: The Depths of Apathy — Detailed Map Specifications
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## Overview: The Total Void
Stratum 7 is the final descent into the core of the protagonist's trauma. The aesthetic is total sensory deprivation—a black void where the only light is the immediate 3x3 grid around the player. There is no music, only the sound of a steady, rhythmic heartbeat and the player's own footsteps. The environment is not made of stone or flesh, but of "Non-Existence."

---

## Floor 31: The Blind Walk
*   **Atmosphere:** Suffocating and silent. The air feels heavy.
*   **Layout:** A large 15x15 grid that is completely invisible.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Audio-Beacon:** A rhythmic pulse that changes pitch based on the player's distance from walls.
*   **Gimmick:** Navigation via audio cues only.
    *   **High-pitched tone:** Player is facing a wall.
    *   **Low rumble:** Player is standing near a pit.
    *   **Silence:** The path is clear.
    *   **One wrong step:** Immediate **Return by Death** to Floor 30.

---

## Floor 32: The Echo of the Strike
*   **Atmosphere:** Tense and violent. The sound of heavy breathing is heard, but no source is visible.
*   **Layout:** A simple square room with invisible walls and hazards.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Invisible Entity:** A monster that only makes sound when it moves or is struck.
*   **Gimmick:** The player must fight the entity using only the **Swing** verb's sound.
    *   **Swoosh:** A miss.
    *   **Wet thud:** A hit.
    *   **Metal clang:** The player hit a wall.
*   **Victory:** The player must push the invisible entity into an invisible spike trap (identified by its low rumble).

---

## Floor 33: The Hall of Apathy
*   **Atmosphere:** Monotonous and infinite.
*   **Layout:** A seemingly endless straight corridor.
*   **Gimmick:** The player must walk in a straight line for 3 consecutive real-time minutes.
    *   **At 45 seconds:** The game simulates a crash (black screen + error sound).
    *   **At 90 seconds:** The game simulates a "Controller Disconnected" message.
    *   **At 135 seconds:** The frame rate drops to 10 FPS and the screen fills with static.
*   **Rule:** Pressing any button other than 'UP' during these events causes a **Return by Death**. The player must ignore the "Glitches" and keep walking toward nothing.

---

## Floor 34: The Final Sacrifice
*   **Atmosphere:** Final and sacrificial. A warm glow emanates from the center of the room.
*   **Layout:** A small circular room with a central furnace.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Furnace of Closure:** A glowing red pit.
    *   **The SWING Block:** A physical block labeled "SWING."
*   **Gimmick:** To open the final door, the player must **Push** the "SWING" block into the furnace and burn it. This permanently removes the **Swing/Attack** verb for the remainder of the game. The player enters the final floor able only to move and push.

---

## Floor 35: The Sovereign of Stasis
*   **Atmosphere:** Absolute stillness. The void becomes a pristine, white floor.
*   **Layout:** A 15x15 grid with the **Sovereign of Stasis** in the center.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Illusion Circle:** Where the Stage 1 illusions appear.
*   **Gimmick:** The Final Confrontation.
    *   **Phase 1 (The Bait):** The boss conjures screaming illusions of Stage 1 characters. **Pushing** them causes a Return by Death.
    *   **Phase 2 (The Truth):** The player must realize the Sovereign has never attacked.
    *   **Phase 3 (The Hostage State):** The UI vanishes. The player is trapped.
*   **Resolution:** 3 continuous real-time minutes of complete inaction (no input) triggers the ending.
