# Stratum 7: The Depths of Apathy — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. The Void’s Residents

### The Echo-Shadow (NPC/Monster)
*   **Aesthetic:** Completely invisible. Its position is only betrayed by the **3x3 Light Circle** (where its silhouette appears as a flickering, distorted disturbance) and its **Heavy Breathing** audio.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Strike:** A sudden, jagged white line that appears in the light circle before the player dies.

### The Sovereign of Stasis (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A towering, monochromatic giant sitting in a meditative pose. His skin looks like cracked obsidian. His face is a hyper-realistic pixel rendering of the protagonist, aged 40 years, with eyes that are closed and weeping gray ink.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Watch:** He does not move. Occasionally, a single crack in his obsidian skin will "bleed" white light.
    *   **The Crumble:** When defeated (by inaction), the obsidian skin shatters and falls away like charcoal, revealing the warm light of the real world behind him.

### The Illusions (Bait)
*   **Aesthetic:** High-saturation, full-color sprites from Stage 1. They look completely out of place in the monochromatic void.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Scream:** Their mouths open wide, and "HELP ME!" text bubbles in various Era-specific fonts (EarthBound, FFX, Persona) explode from their heads.
    *   **The Detonation:** If pushed, they flash red and white before exploding into a shower of "Asset Debris."

## 2. Awakening Room (Phase 4: The Voided State)

### The Absolute Void (Environment)
*   **Description:** The floor is a 3x3 platform made of ash. The bed is gone. The mirror is shattered and shows only darkness.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Gaze:** Giant, monochromatic eyes (The Eldritch Entities) appear in the background, blinking in slow-motion and tracking the player's position.
    *   **The Saving Throw:** Interacting with the "Ash-Bed" causes the protagonist to kneel. The screen flashes white, and a permanent Max HP reduction animation plays.

## 3. Environment Visuals

### The 3x3 Light Circle (Player Aura)
*   **Description:** The only light source. A circular gradient that fades from "Dull Gray" to "Absolute Black."
*   **Animations:** It "flickers" in rhythm with the protagonist's heartbeat. If **Mind** is low, the circle shrinks to 1x1.

### The Hall of Apathy (Floor 33)
*   **Description:** A straight, infinite corridor with walls made of "Fading Memories."
*   **Animations:** As the player walks, the "Simulation Errors" occur. The "Controller Disconnected" screen is a realistic RPG Maker MZ prompt that overlays the entire map.

### The Furnace of Closure (Floor 34)
*   **Description:** A stone pit filled with a warm, golden-orange flame—the only warm color in the entire Stratum.
*   **Animations:** When the "SWING" block is pushed in, the flames roar and turn white, then slowly die down into gray ash.

### The Final Room (Floor 35)
*   **Description:** A pristine, endless white floor. No walls. No grid.
*   **Animations:** The ground is so white it creates a "Glare" effect on the player's monochrome sprite.
