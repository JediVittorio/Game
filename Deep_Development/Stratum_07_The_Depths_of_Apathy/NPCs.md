# Stratum 7: The Depths of Apathy — NPC Profiles
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## The Void’s Echoes

### 1. The Echo-Shadow (The Invisible Stalker)
*   **Visuals:** None. It is completely invisible.
*   **Personality:** Primal and territorial. It exists only to punish noise.
*   **Narrative Role:** The threat of Floor 32. It teaches the player to trust their ears over their eyes.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Heavy Breathing) . . . STOP . . . BREATHING . . ."

### 2. The Sovereign of Stasis (The Core Trauma)
*   **Visuals:** A massive, obsidian-black entity that resembles a frozen explosion. Its "face" is the protagonist's own face, but aged 40 years.
*   **Personality:** Silent and absolute. It doesn't fight; it just *is*. It is the ultimate manifestation of the protagonist's inability to move on.
*   **Narrative Role:** The Final Boss. It represents the "Hostage State" of trauma.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Telepathic Echo) . . . Stay here. In the silence. Where nothing ever changes. Where no one can leave. It’s safer this way."

### 3. The Illusions of Help
*   **Visuals:** Hyper-realistic, full-color versions of the Stage 1 protagonists (Less, Krono, Suji, Maelle, etc.).
*   **Personality:** Desperate and terrified. They scream for the player to save them from a descending spike-ceiling.
*   **Narrative Role:** The bait of Floor 35 Phase 1. They represent the player's conditioned need to "be the hero" and solve things through action.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Screaming) LESS! SAVE ME! DON'T JUST STAND THERE! PUSH ME AWAY! PLEASE!"

## The Real World

### 4. The Trauma Figure (The Resolution)
*   **Visuals:** A person in the protagonist's real bedroom (revealed in the ending).
*   **Narrative Role:** The reason for the simulation. Their identity is the "Unbearable Truth."
*   **Identity:** Revealed to be the protagonist's [Partner/Parent/Child] who is no longer present.
*   **Dialogue:** "Still here. Remembered everything this time. Think I'm ready to go get some air."
