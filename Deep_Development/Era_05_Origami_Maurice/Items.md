# Era 5: Origami Maurice — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Weapons (Tools of Flatness)

### The Oversized Hammer
*   **Description:** A giant wooden mallet that looks like it belongs in a cartoon. It squeaks on impact.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Maurice’s primary weapon. It deals 2x damage to cardboard and 100x damage to the player's dignity."
*   **Effect:** Heavy physical damage. Can "Flatten" the enemy (reducing their evasion but also their target size).

### The Spiked Boot
*   **Description:** A leather boot with a single, massive iron spike protruding from the sole.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Perfect for jumping on enemies who have hair. Don't use it on fire. Or spikes. Or feelings."
*   **Effect:** Ranged physical attack (Jump). Deals bonus damage to "Squishy" enemies.

### The Encyclopedia of Failures
*   **Description:** A massive, leather-bound book that Shell-don uses as a shield.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Contains the names of every player who has ever reached a 'Game Over' screen in this era. It’s very heavy."
*   **Effect:** Increases Defense. Can be "Thrown" to deal magic damage based on Shell-don's current HP.

---

## 2. Armor & Accessories (Protective Folds)

### The Felt Overalls
*   **Description:** Red overalls made of thick, soft felt.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Provides a +20% resistance to papercuts. It also makes you look like you’re ready for a nap."
*   **Effect:** Increases Defense and Max HP.

### The Humidity Shield
*   **Description:** A plastic poncho that is slightly too small for a turtle.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Protects the wearer from becoming 'Soggy.' Absolutely necessary for the Under-Folder dungeon."
*   **Effect:** Immunity to "Soggy" and "Damp" status effects.

### The Audience Badge
*   **Description:** A shiny gold star that says "I AM ENTERTAINING."
*   **Flavor Text:** "Increases the amount of 'Star Power' generated by successful QTEs. The audience loves a winner."
*   **Effect:** Increases Star Power gain by 50%.

---

## 3. Consumables (Stationery Sustenance)

### Library Paste
*   **Description:** A jar of white, sticky goo.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Tastes like childhood and chemical regret. It’s the primary healing item for paper entities."
*   **Effect:** Restores 100 HP. Inflicts "Sticky" (reduces speed for 1 turn).

### Fresh Cardstock
*   **Description:** A pristine, 110lb sheet of white paper.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Eating this gives you a temporary 'Rigidity' buff. You feel crisp, clean, and impossible to fold."
*   **Effect:** Increases Defense by 50% for 3 turns.

### The Script of Second Chances
*   **Description:** A crumpled piece of paper with a handwritten line of dialogue.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Use this in battle to force the audience to stop booing. It grants one 'Free Turn' by distracting the critics."
*   **Effect:** Halts enemy actions for 1 turn.

---

## 4. Key Items (Millennial Artifacts)

### The Millennial Marker
*   **Description:** A thick, black permanent marker.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Used to 'draw' the Millennial Portal onto the cardboard fortress. It smells like a productive afternoon."

### The Crystal Star (Satire)
*   **Description:** A plastic star that looks like a Christmas tree topper.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It doesn't grant wishes; it just glows slightly when you’re near a plot hole. It’s a very useful tool for a narrator."
