# Era 5: Origami Maurice — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### Maurice (The Mute Plumber)
*   **Aesthetic:** 2D "Paper" style. Flat colors, thick outlines. He should appear to be made of two separate layers of paper glued together.
*   **Idle:** He stands with his chest puffed out, swaying slightly like a piece of paper in a breeze.
*   **Walking:** A "Paper-Walk." He remains flat, and when he changes direction, he performs a "flip" animation that shows his zero-thickness side.
*   **Attack:** "The Fold." He folds himself into a sharp point and dive-bombs the enemy.
*   **Victory:** He folds himself into a paper swan and floats off-screen.

### Shell-don (The Anxious Researcher)
*   **Aesthetic:** A rounder, more "cardboard" look. His shell has visible texture and a "recycled" symbol on the bottom.
*   **Idle:** He is constantly peering over his glasses at a massive book.
*   **Walking:** A slow, deliberate waddle. His book bounces on his back.
*   **Attack:** "Tattle-Tale." He slams his book shut, creating a "Shockwave of Information" that deals damage and reveals HP.
*   **Victory:** He wipes his glasses, puts his book away, and lets out a visible sigh of relief.

---

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### The Shadow Cardboard (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A massive, towering silhouette that looks like a box has come to life. Its edges are frayed and "fuzzy."
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** It remains inside its box, only its glowing purple eyes are visible. It attacks by "closing its lids" on the player.
    *   **Phase 2:** The box unfolds, revealing a lanky, jagged entity made of dark paper. It uses "Paper-Cuts" (quick slashing attacks).
    *   **Phase 3:** It begins to "Wilt." It turns a soggy brown color and its movements become slow and fluid.

### The Art Critic (Common NPC/Enemy)
*   **Aesthetic:** A very tall, thin sprite with a monocle and a long scarf. He looks like a piece of parchment that was stretched too thin.
*   **Animations:** He "attacks" by pointing a long finger and producing a speech bubble with a "0/10."

---

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **Flaming Anvil:** A 2D sprite of a 3D anvil, painted in bright, "fake" colors. It falls vertically and creates a "Flattened" sprite of the character it hits.
*   **Faded Goomba:** A mushroom-man with a "washed-out" texture. It moves in a jittery, frame-skipping pattern.
*   **Existential Cardboard:** A simple brown cube that occasionally opens up to show a pair of weeping eyes inside.

---

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Objects:**
    *   **The Stage Lights:** These track the active character, casting a circular light on the "floor."
    *   **The Scenery:** Painted bushes and clouds that are held up by visible wooden sticks. They occasionally wobble or fall over.
    *   **The Audience:** A background layer of silhouettes that "bob" up and down and throw items (anvils, sandwiches, flowers) onto the stage.
    *   **The Tape:** Peeling pieces of masking tape on buildings that flutter in the "wind."
