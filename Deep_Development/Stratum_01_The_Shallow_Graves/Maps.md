# Stratum 1: The Shallow Graves — Detailed Map Specifications
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## Overview: The Grayscale Echo
Stratum 1 is a 15x15 grid-based descent through the "Shallow Graves" of the protagonist's memories. The aesthetic is a stark, monochromatic distortion of the Stage 1 overworld. Familiar landmarks from Era 1 are here, but they are twisted, pixelated, and "wrong." There is no music—only the sound of a distant heartbeat and the crunch of gray grass underfoot.

---

## Floor 1: The Re-Education
*   **Atmosphere:** Heavy and pedagogical. The air feels thick with the weight of things forgotten.
*   **Layout:** A simple path blocked by a massive, gray slab of stone.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Slab of Compacted Memory:** A 2x2 block that requires the **Push** verb to move. It is covered in the faded names of childhood friends.
    *   **The Pressure Plate:** A tile that glows with a faint, white light when the slab is correctly positioned.
*   **Gameplay Intro:** This floor introduces the **Push** verb. The **Lost Guide NPC** stands at the exit, watching.

---

## Floor 2: The Phantom Strike
*   **Atmosphere:** Tense. The shadows seem to reach for the player.
*   **Layout:** A corridor filled with "Corrupted Flesh"—thick, pulsing vines of gray data that block the path.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Watcher:** A stationary, invincible entity with a giant eye. It fires a beam of "Erasure" if it sees the player.
    *   **Cover Blocks:** Small slabs that provide protection from the Watcher’s gaze.
*   **Gameplay Intro:** This floor introduces the **Swing** verb. The player must slash the corrupted flesh to create a path, then time their movements between cover to avoid the Watcher.

---

## Floor 3: The Mandatory Sacrifice
*   **Atmosphere:** Sudden and violent. The floor is made of "Fragile Memory" tiles.
*   **Layout:** A wide room with a single door on the opposite side.
*   **Gimmick:** The floor is designed to collapse. On the first entry, it is mathematically impossible to reach the door. The floor falls, and the player experiences their first **Return by Death**.
*   **The Second Loop:** On Loop 2, a **Petrified Stage 1 NPC** (an Onett citizen) appears near the entrance. The player must **Push** the petrified citizen onto the fragile tiles to "test" the path, sacrificing the memory to cross.

---

## Floor 4: The Rhythm of Guilt
*   **Atmosphere:** Chaotic. The sound of whistling wind is periodically interrupted by the *thwack* of arrows hitting stone.
*   **Layout:** A complex maze of invisible arrow traps.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Trigger Coordinates:** These are arranged in the exact attack pattern of the Era 1 final boss (Mr. Adulthood/Giygas callback).
*   **Gimmick:** The traps are invisible. The player must die repeatedly to "map" the safe path. The death screen for this floor contains a hidden password written in blood.

---

## Floor 5: Checkpoint 1 — The Awakening Room (Phase 0)
*   **Atmosphere:** Pristine, silent, and orderly. It is a grayscale replica of the protagonist’s real bedroom.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Bed:** A traditional save point that restores HP/MP fully (for now).
    *   **The Mirror:** Shows the protagonist's face clearly. No distortion.
*   **NPC Placement:** **The Glitch Barista** stands in the corner, offering a cup of "Decaf Despair" and cryptic warnings. She is the only point of stability in the descent.
