# Stratum 1: The Shallow Graves — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. The Protagonist of Anguish

### The Hollow Hero (Stage 2 Sprite)
*   **Aesthetic:** A monochrome version of the Stage 1 protagonist. His eyes are hollow pits. His movements are slow and deliberate, as if every step requires a conscious effort.
*   **Animations:**
    *   **The Swing:** A slow, heavy arc with the Rusted Blade. The sound is a metallic "ting" if it hits stone, and a "squelch" if it hits flesh.
    *   **The Push:** The protagonist leans into the object, his feet digging into the ground. A visible "Guilt Aura" (gray smoke) emanates from his hands.
    *   **The Return:** When the player dies, the sprite "fractures" like glass and is then "sucked" upward into a red-and-black vortex.

---

## 2. Purgatorial Sprites

### The Lost Guide (NPC)
*   **Aesthetic:** A floating, featureless gray robe. It has no physical limbs, but the robe "bulges" as if there is a body inside it.
*   **Animations:** It moves without walking, simply drifting across the grid. When it speaks, the robe "pulses" in rhythm with the dialogue box.

### The Glitch Barista (Anchor NPC)
*   **Aesthetic:** A high-contrast sprite that constantly "jitter-shifts" between different art styles. One frame she is a detailed portrait; the next she is a 4-color NES sprite.
*   **Animations:** She is the only sprite with a warm, "living" animation. She wipes a counter and pours coffee with a fluidity that is unnerving compared to the rest of the world.

---

## 3. Hazards & Horrors

### The Watcher (Entity)
*   **Aesthetic:** A giant, stone eye embedded in a wall or floating in mid-air. The iris is a swirling vortex of black ink.
*   **Animations:** It slowly rotates to track the player's position. When it "sees" the player, the pupil dilates, and a red laser-line connects it to the protagonist before the "Erasure" beam fires.

### Corrupted Flesh (Environment)
*   **Aesthetic:** Thick, pulsating gray vines that have "veins" of blue data running through them.
*   **Animations:** They "breathe" slowly. When slashed with the Rusted Blade, they bleed pixelated gray blocks that evaporate.

---

## 4. Environment Visuals

### The Fragile Memory Tiles (Floor 3)
*   **Description:** Tiles that look like the sidewalks of Suburbia Prime (Era 1) but are cracked and "glitching."
*   **Animations:** When stepped on, they flash red and then "fall" into the void with a sound like a hard drive crashing.

### The Awakening Room (Phase 0)
*   **Description:** A perfectly still, grayscale bedroom.
*   **Animations:** 
    *   **The Mirror:** Shows a clear, non-glitched version of the protagonist.
    *   **The Bed:** When interacted with, the screen fades to black with a "Save Successful" sound that is slightly distorted.
    *   **The Window:** Shows a constant, unchanging beige void outside.
