# Stratum 1: The Shallow Graves — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. The Fundamental Verbs (Equippable Items)

### The Rusted Blade (Swing)
*   **Description:** A dull, notched sword that hums with a faint, mournful vibration. It smells like iron and old blood.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It was once 'Justice.' Now it is just a tool for cutting through the things you’d rather forget. It makes a wet sound when it strikes flesh."
*   **Effect:** Cuts through **Corrupted Flesh**. Can be used to "Test" walls for false architecture.

### The Weight of Guilt (Push)
*   **Description:** Not a physical item, but a "burden" the player carries. It manifests as a heavy aura around the player’s hands.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The ability to move the immovable. The more you push, the slower you become. Every inch gained is a pound of guilt added."
*   **Effect:** Allows the player to move 2x2 and 1x1 blocks. Reduces movement speed by 50% during the action.

---

## 2. Sentimental Objects (Puzzle Keys)

### The Faded Photograph
*   **Description:** A small, grayscale photo of a family dinner. The faces have been scratched out with a red pen.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A memory of warmth that has been meticulously erased. Carrying it makes your heart feel heavy, but it opens the door to the Commercial District."
*   **Effect:** Used as a key for the Floor 5 gate.

### The Stolen Spoon (Era 9 Callback)
*   **Description:** A simple silver spoon, tarnished and bent.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A relic from the 'Sandbox of Thirst.' It represents a minor crime that has blossomed into a major regret."
*   **Effect:** Can be "Sacrificed" to a Watcher to temporarily blind it.

---

## 3. Consumables (Purgatorial Sustenance)

### Decaf Despair
*   **Description:** A paper cup filled with a liquid that is exactly room temperature. It has no flavor.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Served by the Glitch Barista. It doesn't give you energy; it just makes the silence a little more bearable."
*   **Effect:** Restores 5 **Mind** resource (Variable 706).

### A Handful of Gray Dirt
*   **Description:** Dirt collected from the Shallow Graves.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It’s gritty, it’s dry, and it tastes like the end of a Tuesday. Eating it provides a temporary 'Numbness' buff."
*   **Effect:** Increases Defense for 3 turns. Inflicts "Lethargy."
