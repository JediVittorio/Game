# Stratum 1: The Shallow Graves — NPC Profiles
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## The Purgatorial Denizens

### 1. The Lost Guide (The Pedagogue)
*   **Visuals:** A faceless figure in a gray robe. He has no arms, and his movements are floating and ghostly.
*   **Personality:** He speaks in flat, monotone sentences. He views the player’s struggle as a "necessary re-education."
*   **Narrative Role:** He introduces the **Push** verb. If the player strikes him with the sword, he transforms into a "Minor Horror" and kills the player instantly, teaching the first lesson: violence has consequences.
*   **Dialogue:** "The weight of your choices is physical here. Push. Do not strike. To strike is to end the lesson prematurely."

### 2. The Petrified Onett Citizen (The Sacrifice)
*   **Visuals:** A grayscale, stone-like sprite of a generic citizen from Era 1. They are frozen in a pose of mild confusion, holding a shopping bag.
*   **Personality:** None. They are a "coagulated mass of repressed memory."
*   **Narrative Role:** They are used as a physical tool to solve the Floor 3 puzzle.
*   **Satire:** Their presence is a cruel callback to the lighthearted nature of Stage 1. They are literally a "prop" in the protagonist's descent.

### 3. The Glitch Barista (The Anchor)
*   **Visuals:** A woman in a standard barista uniform, but her sprite "flickers" between 8-bit and high-def styles. She has a warm, inviting smile that never reaches her eyes.
*   **Personality:** Helpful, calm, and seemingly immune to the horror of the labyrinth. She sells nothing and offers no mechanical benefit, but her presence provides a small "Sanity" buff.
*   **Narrative Role:** The only NPC in Stage 2 who never becomes hostile. She stands at every 5-floor checkpoint.
*   **Dialogue:** "Welcome to the first anchor. Your memory is holding up well, all things considered. Would you like a cup of something silent?"

## Common Hazards (The Graves' Dwellers)

*   **Watcher:** A stationary entity that looks like a giant, stone eye. It doesn't move, but its gaze is fatal.
*   **Corrupted Flesh:** Not a sentient creature, but a pulsing vine that reacts to the player's presence by growing faster. It must be "pruned" with the **Swing** verb.
*   **Arrow Trap:** An invisible event that fires a physical projectile. It represents "The Sting of Regret."
*   **The Void-Shadow:** A fast-moving silhouette that only appears when the player remains stationary for more than 5 seconds. It is a manifestation of "Stagnation."
