# Era 6: Extraterrestrial-COM 2 — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### Commander Rick (The Recon Ranger)
*   **Aesthetic:** Tactical and gritty. High-resolution pixel art with visible battle-scars. His armor is a mix of green and black.
*   **Idle:** He has a noticeable twitch in his left eye. He is constantly checking his shotgun, which occasionally slips from his hand.
*   **Walking:** A low, tactical crouch-walk. He moves as if he’s expecting to be shot at any second (he is).
*   **Attack:** "Close-Range Whiff." He points his shotgun at the enemy’s face. The screen flashes white. The enemy takes 0 damage. A giant red "MISS" appears. Rick falls to his knees and screams at the sky.
*   **Victory:** He pulls out a bottle of whiskey, pours it in his eyes, and gives a thumbs-up while tears (of pain) stream down his face.

### Vera (The Field Specialist)
*   **Aesthetic:** Covered in more bandages than armor. Her medic kit is open, and various pills and syringes are spilling out.
*   **Idle:** She is patting her drone, "Despair," which is floating next to her. The drone occasionally lets out a puff of black smoke.
*   **Walking:** A frantic, hurried run. She looks like she’s trying to reach someone before they bleed out.
*   **Attack:** "Emotional Stability Beam." Her drone fires a beam of rainbow light at a teammate. The teammate’s head expands and glows with a "Hope" animation.
*   **Victory:** She sits on a folding chair and starts doing rhythmic breathing exercises.

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### The Chosen Auditor (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A tall, purple alien in a perfectly tailored tuxedo. He floats above a platform made of floating hexagonal spreadsheets.
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** He uses his clipboard to "Audit" the player, which manifests as a beam of red tape that binds characters.
    *   **Phase 2:** He summons "Reinforcements" (which are just copies of the player’s own character, but more successful).
    *   **Phase 3:** He transforms into a giant, multi-limbed calculator that tries to "Solve" the player out of existence.

### Socially Awkward Sectoid (Common NPC/Enemy)
*   **Aesthetic:** A small, gray alien with huge, watery eyes. It wears a tiny bow tie and a sweater vest.
*   **Animations:** It avoids eye contact. When it "attacks," it looks like it’s trying to tell a joke but forgets the punchline.

---

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **ADVENT Grunt:** A sleek, red-and-black armored soldier. It moves in a rigid, robotic pattern. When it dies, it lets out a sound like a deflating balloon.
*   **Panic-Inducing Drone:** A small, metallic eye with propellers. It emits a visible "Fear Frequency" (purple waves).
*   **Statistical Nightmare:** A flickering, glitchy entity that changes its shape between a "1" and a "0" rapidly.

---

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Objects:**
    *   **The Tactical Grid:** A glowing blue overlay that pulses with the music. It turns red in areas with high danger.
    *   **The Half-Cover:** Benches and crates that crumble and pixelate when hit by "Missed" shots.
    *   **The Extraction Beacon:** A column of green light that has "SAFE ZONE" written in alien text on it.
    *   **Surveillance Cameras:** Small, rotating drones that cast a red "Detection Zone" on the floor. If the player enters, the camera lets out a siren sound and spawns a "Slightly Annoyed" alien.
