# Era 6: Extraterrestrial-COM 2 — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Weapons (Tools of Statistical Despair)

### The "Close-Range" Shotgun
*   **Description:** A high-impact shotgun with a barrel that is exactly 2 inches long.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Rick’s primary weapon. It has a 99% hit chance at point-blank range, which means it misses exactly when you need it most."
*   **Effect:** High physical damage. Accuracy decreases by 50% if the target is more than 3 tiles away.

### The Emotional Support Sword
*   **Description:** A glowing blue katana that hums with a frequency that is supposed to be "calming."
*   **Flavor Text:** "You don't swing it to kill; you swing it to feel like you're in control of your life. It smells like ozone and lavender."
*   **Effect:** Can "Parry" projectiles. Successful hits reduce the user's "Panic" meter.

### The Med-Drone "Despair"
*   **Description:** A small, floating robot that emits a soft, mournful beep.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Vera’s drone. It doesn't just heal physical wounds; it also provides a 5-minute therapy session (during combat)."
*   **Effect:** Restores HP. Can "Cure" the "Panic" and "Existential Crisis" status effects.

## 2. Armor & Accessories (Protective Gaslighting)

### The Panic-Proof Vest
*   **Description:** A heavy, black vest lined with noise-canceling foam and pictures of puppies.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Makes the wearer feel safe and loved, even when an alien is attempting to consume their memories."
*   **Effect:** Increases Defense. Grants immunity to the "Loud Noises" debuff.

### The Gaslight Goggles
*   **Description:** High-tech glasses that display a permanent "100%" over every enemy's head.
*   **Flavor Text:** "They don't actually change the probability; they just change how the user *perceives* the probability. Ignorance is tactical."
*   **Effect:** Increases Accuracy by 10% (Perceived). Increases the chance of "Mental Breakdown" when a shot misses.

### The 99% Lucky Charm
*   **Description:** A small, four-leaf clover made of discarded alien alloy.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Found in a trash can outside the ADVENT city center. It feels heavy with the irony of its own existence."
*   **Effect:** Increases Luck by 50. Has a 1% chance to instantly kill the user if they miss a 99% shot.

---

## 3. Consumables (Tactical Sustenance)

### Whiskey Eye-Drops
*   **Description:** A small dropper bottle filled with high-proof bourbon and a bit of blue alien blood.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Rick’s secret to 'Tactical Vision.' It burns like a thousand suns, but it makes the aliens look slightly more vulnerable."
*   **Effect:** Increases Crit Rate for 3 turns. Inflicts "Blurry Vision" (Accuracy -10%).

### Hope-in-a-Can
*   **Description:** A pressurized can of aerosolized optimism.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Spray liberally on any soldier who has fallen to their knees to question physics. It tastes like blueberry and lies."
*   **Effect:** Restores 50 MP. Cures "Existential Crisis."

### Probability Potion
*   **Description:** A swirling, purple liquid that seems to defy the laws of gravity.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Drinking this makes the user exist in multiple timelines simultaneously. In at least one of them, the shot probably hit."
*   **Effect:** Grants the "Precognition" buff (Evasion +20%).

## 4. Key Items (Tactical Documents)

### The 100% Hit-Chance Token
*   **Description:** A golden coin that glows with a blinding, divine light.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A legendary artifact that guarantees a hit. Upon closer inspection, it’s a fake. The '100' is just two '0's and a '1' drawn in marker."

### The Avatar-ish Project Blueprints
*   **Description:** A series of complex diagrams showing an alien that is also a sofa.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The aliens' master plan for global domination involves making humanity too comfortable to resist. Diabolical."
