# Stratum 4: The Interface of Atrophy — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Meta-Items (UI as Geometry)

### The Displaced HP Bar (Pushable Object)
*   **Description:** A long, segmented bar made of glowing green glass. It has a tiny "HP" icon at one end and pulses with a rhythmic light.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It was once your lifeblood. Now it’s just a 50-pound weight you have to drag across a pressure plate. The screen feels emptier without it, but the door is open."
*   **Effect:** A 2x2 pushable block found on Floor 16. Using it permanently reduces Max HP by 20%.

### The Settings Slider: Brightness
*   **Description:** A metal rail with a glowing white "Tab" that can be slid back and forth.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A physical manifestation of a software variable. Moving it doesn't just change the light; it changes the amount of noise you make in the code. High visibility, high risk."
*   **Effect:** A 1x1 block used on Floor 18. Pushing it to the right increases screen tint but also increases enemy aggro radius.

### The Corrupted Save File (3.5" Disk)
*   **Description:** A giant, pixelated floppy disk with a "Write Protect" tab that has been snapped off. It’s leaking a black, ink-like fluid.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The container of your history. To move forward, you must feed it to the Erase Terminal. You won't be able to go back, but perhaps that’s the point."
*   **Effect:** A 2x2 pushable block used on Floor 19. Pushing it into the terminal permanently removes the 'Save' menu option.

## 2. Technical Tools (Glitch Gear)

### The Kill Command
*   **Description:** A scroll containing a single line of code: `taskkill /f /im PROTAGONIST.EXE`.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A desperate measure for a desperate situation. It doesn't kill you; it kills the process that is keeping the door locked. It’s highly unstable."
*   **Effect:** Used on Floor 17 to unlock the exit while the 'Hostile Pause' is active.

### Hex-Buffer (Consumable)
*   **Description:** A small, hexagonal pill that tastes like static electricity and ozone.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Provides a temporary layer of protection against 'Scanline Storms.' It makes your thoughts feel segmented and orderly."
*   **Effect:** Grants 3 turns of immunity to **Mind** drain.

### RAM Shard (Consumable)
*   **Description:** A glowing sliver of green silicon.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A fragment of short-term memory. Using it allows you to 'Recall' a previous frame, effectively undoing your last movement."
*   **Effect:** Resets the player's position on the map to where they were 1 turn ago.

## 3. Passive Protection (The User's Shield)

### Glitch-Resistant Vest
*   **Description:** A tactical vest made of woven "Null-Data" fibers. It flickers between existence and non-existence every second.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It’s hard for the simulation to delete you if it can't quite find your coordinates. Provides excellent defense against 'Erase Commands.'"
*   **Effect:** 25% chance to negate any "Asset Deletion" attack from the Architect.

### The Latency Earring
*   **Description:** A small jewelry piece that looks like a spinning "Loading" icon.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It slows down your perception of time, allowing you to react to 'Hanging Assets' even when the game is lagging."
*   **Effect:** Increases the window for reaction-based puzzles by 10 frames.
