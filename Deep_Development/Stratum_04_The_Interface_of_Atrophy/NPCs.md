# Stratum 4: The Interface of Atrophy — NPC Profiles
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## The Architectural Entities

### 1. The Blind Oracle (The User Guide)
*   **Visuals:** A figure composed of hundreds of flickering white pixels. He has no eyes, only a scrolling text-line that reads "SYSTEM WARNING" across his face.
*   **Personality:** Calm and informative. He views the player not as a hero, but as a "User" who is making catastrophic errors.
*   **Narrative Role:** He explains the mechanics of the "Brightness Slider" and the "Beast of Silence."
*   **Dialogue:** "The light reveals the path, User. But the light also reveals your heartbeat to the things that prefer the dark. Do not move unless you are certain you can be seen. Or better yet... do not move at all."

### 2. The Architect of Atrophy (The Governing Presence)
*   **Visuals:** A towering entity made of floating hexadecimal code (0-F). It doesn't have a body; it is a "Vortex of Instructions."
*   **Personality:** Indifferent and systematic. It exists solely to "Prune" the simulation of unnecessary elements (like the player’s comfort).
*   **Narrative Role:** The primary antagonist of the stratum. It doesn't fight; it "Deletes."
*   **Dialogue:** "Resource management is the core of any stable system. You have too many options. Let us begin the pruning. Which part of your agency will you surrender first?"

### 3. The Beast of Silence (Entity)
*   **Visuals:** A long, spindly creature with many ears and no mouth. It is invisible in the dark and only appears as a silhouette in the light.
*   **Narrative Role:** The hunter of Floor 18. It forces the player to manage their brightness level carefully.

---

## Common Hazards (The Rot's Claws)

*   **Hanging Asset:** A monster that looks like a 3D model that hasn't finished rendering (T-posing). It drops from the ceiling to deal heavy damage.
*   **Hex-Void:** A pit that looks like a hole in the game's code. Falling in triggers a Return by Death and a permanent -2 reduction to Max HP.
*   **Scanline Storm:** A screen effect that reduces visibility and deals constant damage to **Mind**.
*   **Erase Command:** A projectile fired by the Architect that, if it hits, deletes a random non-key item from the player's inventory.
