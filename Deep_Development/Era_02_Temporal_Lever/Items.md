# Era 2: Temporal Lever — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Weapons (Tools of Temporal Vandalism)

### The Property Damage Katana
*   **Description:** A sleek, curved blade that is surprisingly heavy. It has a small counter on the hilt that tracks "Furniture Destroyed."
*   **Flavor Text:** "Krono’s primary tool for communication. It deals 2x damage to pots, crates, and silent NPCs."
*   **Effect:** High physical damage. Increases the chance of finding loot in breakable objects.

### The Hostile Hammer
*   **Description:** A massive mallet created by Ruca. It has a tiny, angry face painted on the head.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It was supposed to be a 'Safety Hammer,' but it developed a personality and decided that 'Safety' is for the weak."
*   **Effect:** Deals blunt damage. Has a 5% chance to hit the user instead of the enemy.

### The Alimony Blade
*   **Description:** A rusted broadsword that is being held together by legal documents and dried swamp-mud.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Toad’s sword. It’s heavy with the weight of missed payments and lost custody battles."
*   **Effect:** Damage increases as the player’s gold decreases.

## 2. Armor & Accessories (Temporal Protection)

### The Bug-Stomping Boot
*   **Description:** A heavy, prehistoric leather boot with a very flat sole.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Perfect for altering the entire course of human evolution with a single, careless step."
*   **Effect:** Increases critical hit rate but permanently alters the "Future" tileset when worn in 65M BC.

### The Epoch Watch
*   **Description:** A pocket watch that runs backward.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Tells you exactly how much time you’ve wasted on unskippable cutscenes."
*   **Effect:** Grants a "Precognition" buff, slightly increasing evasion.

### Alimony Papers (Toad Accessory)
*   **Description:** A stack of damp, ink-smudged scrolls.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Makes the wearer feel so much psychic pain that physical pain is barely noticeable."
*   **Effect:** Increases Defense by 50% but reduces Luck to zero.

---

## 3. Consumables (The Flavors of Time)

### High-Fructose Tomato Juice
*   **Description:** A bottle of bright red, pulpy liquid. It’s dangerously sweet.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The nectar of the 2300 AD utopia. It provides energy, joy, and a mild existential dread regarding your own mortality."
*   **Effect:** Restores 100 MP.

### Primitive Jerky
*   **Description:** A piece of meat that is 90% gristle and 10% prehistoric dust.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It’s tough, it’s salty, and it’s been extinct for millions of years. Chewing it is its own workout."
*   **Effect:** Restores 100 HP. Increases Strength for 3 turns.

### Agricultural Blight (Sabotage Item)
*   **Description:** A vial of gray, foul-smelling dust.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The only way to ruin a perfect tomato utopia. Sprinkle liberally on hydroponic tubes."
*   **Effect:** Used in the "Tomato Boss" fight to disable his healing abilities.

## 4. Key Items (Timeline Manipulation)

### The Gate Key (Beta Version)
*   **Description:** A small, pulsating crystal that emits a sound like a dial-up modem.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Ruca’s first prototype. It opens temporal rifts and occasionally summons a very confused pizza delivery man from 1997."

### The Golden Beetle
*   **Description:** A preserved prehistoric bug.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The literal catalyst for bipedal tomato dominance. Carrying it makes you feel like the most important person in the history of the world, and also the most careless."
