# Era 2: Temporal Lever — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### Krono (The Mute Vandal)
*   **Aesthetic:** 16-bit, vibrant colors (Red hair, Blue tunic, White pants). High-contrast shading.
*   **Idle:** He stands with his hand on the hilt of his katana, his hair gently swaying as if in a perpetual, heroic breeze.
*   **Walking:** A fluid, confident stride. When he walks past breakable objects, he has a 10% chance to perform a "Drive-by Slash" animation.
*   **Attack:** A blur of steel. He draws, strikes, and sheathes his blade so fast that the damage numbers appear before the animation is finished.
*   **Victory:** He crosses his arms and nods silently while a shower of confetti (actually broken pottery shards) falls from above.

### Ruca (The Malicious Inventor)
*   **Aesthetic:** Purple hair, yellow vest, and oversized glasses that reflect the UI elements.
*   **Idle:** She is frantically hitting a small, sparking machine with a hammer. The machine occasionally bites her hand.
*   **Walking:** A brisk walk, occasionally glancing at a handheld scanner that is emitting red alerts.
*   **Attack:** She swings her "Hostile Hammer." On a "Miss," the hammer spins around and hits Ruca in the head, causing a "Confusion" animation.
*   **Victory:** She accidentally triggers a fireworks display that singes her eyebrows.

### Toad (The Alimony Knight)
*   **Aesthetic:** Green skin, golden armor, and a long, flowing green cape. His eyes are large and perpetually watery.
*   **Idle:** He sighs deeply, with a "thought bubble" showing a tiny picture of a house with a "FOR SALE" sign.
*   **Walking:** A heavy, burdened hop-walk. His broadsword drags on the ground, creating sparks.
*   **Attack:** A massive, clumsy overhead swing. He lets out a ribbit of despair with every strike.
*   **Victory:** He sits down and starts reading a stack of legal scrolls while sobbing.

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### The Tomato Citizen (Common NPC/Enemy)
*   **Aesthetic:** A bright red sphere with thin, spindly arms and legs. They wear futuristic silver hats and tiny sunglasses.
*   **Animations:** They roll when they move fast. They attack by spraying "High-Pressure Juice" from their hats.
*   **Defeat:** They pop like a balloon, leaving a red stain on the map that lasts for 10 seconds.

### The Disgruntled Tomato Farmer (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A massive, multi-tentacled cosmic horror that is roughly the size of a barn. It wears a giant, tattered straw hat and denim overalls that are straining at the seams.
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** He operates a giant "Hover-Tractor" that has laser-plows. He attacks by "harvesting" the player.
    *   **Phase 2:** The tractor explodes. He fights on foot (tentacle?), using a pitchfork that is also a sniper rifle.
    *   **Phase 3:** He enters "Blight Mode." His skin turns a sickly gray-green, and he attacks by vomiting clouds of spores.

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **Existential Lizard:** A raptor with a monocle and a thinking-cap. It pauses mid-attack to look at its own claws in horror.
*   **Sentient Fertilizer:** A green slime variant that has tiny flowers growing out of its head. It attacks by "hugging" the player.
*   **Tomato Enforcer:** A larger, muscular tomato with a "POLICE" sash. It carries a glowing baton.

---

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Objects:**
    *   **The Time Gate:** A swirling blue vortex that occasionally spits out random objects (a boot, a slice of pizza, a lawsuit).
    *   **Prehistoric Insects:** Gold-colored beetles that crawl across the screen. If the player steps on one, the screen flashes red for a frame.
    *   **Tomato Utopia Buildings:** Sleek, organic-looking towers that pulse with a faint red light. They have "Self-Watering" animations.
