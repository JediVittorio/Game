# Era 2: Temporal Lever — NPC Profiles
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## The Protagonists (The Out-of-Time Trio)

### 1. Krono (The Mute Vandal)
*   **Visuals:** A boy with spiky red hair that looks like it could pop a balloon from ten feet away. He carries a katana and wears a tunic that is aggressively blue.
*   **Personality:** Krono is strictly mute. He communicates his thoughts, desires, and tactical advice entirely through the destruction of pots, crates, and nearby scenery. A double-nod followed by the smashing of a table means "I agree, we should proceed."
*   **Narrative Role:** The "hero" who is essentially a force of nature. He doesn't have a motivation; he just happens to be in every time period where something is breakable.
*   **Satirical Twist:** His "Awakening" occurs when he realizes he can't be sued for property damage if the plaintiff hasn't been born yet.

### 2. Ruca (The Malicious Inventor)
*   **Visuals:** A young woman with purple hair and large, circular glasses. She carries a hammer and a series of devices that look like they were designed by someone who hates the concept of safety.
*   **Personality:** Ruca is a "genius" whose inventions are sentient and possess a deep-seated grudge against the player. Her teleporter didn't malfunction; it was just trying to get away from her.
*   **Narrative Role:** The source of the group's temporal transport and most of their physical injuries. She speaks in upbeat, optimistic tones while her latest robot tries to strangle the party.

### 3. Toad (The Alimony Knight)
*   **Visuals:** A bipedal, anthropomorphic frog wearing a green cape and carrying a massive broadsword. He has a perpetually worried expression.
*   **Personality:** A former knight who was transformed into a frog by a dark wizard. He doesn't mind the skin; he minds the fact that his transformation was used as a legal loophole in his divorce proceedings.
*   **Narrative Role:** The "noble" warrior who spends most of the dialogue complaining about child support payments and the rising cost of pond-side real estate.
*   **Satirical Twist:** His "Ultimate Technique" involves summoning a legal team to issue a restraining order against the enemy.

## Major NPCs

### 4. Ayla-Z (The Temporal Cavewoman)
*   **Description:** A prehistoric woman wearing a leopard-print lab coat. She carries a club and a sophisticated temporal scanner.
*   **Role:** The guide to the prehistoric era. She is the only person who understands that stepping on a bug is a bad idea.
*   **Dialogue:** "Strong spirit! Small brain! Bug is friend of future! Stomp bug, future become red and round! Bad salsa! No stomp!"

### 5. The Tomato High Priest
*   **Description:** A massive, particularly juicy tomato in 2300 AD wearing a futuristic mitre. He rolls around on a golden hover-disk.
*   **Role:** The leader of the Tomato Utopia. He views humans as "Unstructured Salsa."
*   **Dialogue:** "The Great Catalyst was stomped so that we might roll! To be bipedal is to be divine! Why do you look at us with hunger, meat-creature?"

### 6. The Disgruntled Tomato Farmer (The Boss)
*   **Description:** A towering, eldritch parasite from the stars, now wearing overalls and a straw hat. He is exhausted and just wants to grow his award-winning beefsteaks in peace.
*   **Role:** The "Villain" of the era. He represents the soul-crushing reality of agricultural labor.
*   **Dialogue:** "I was going to consume the planet's life force... but then I found the joy of hydroponics. Now you show up with your 'swords' and your 'magic' and you're stepping on my mulch? That’s it. The harvest is cancelled!"

## Common Enemies (The Temporal Anomalies)

*   **Existential Lizard:** A prehistoric raptor that stops mid-hunt to ponder the futility of its own extinction.
*   **Sentient Fertilizer:** A pile of glowing goop that attacks by trying to "nurture" the player into a plant.
*   **Paradox Poodle:** A dog that exists in three time periods at once, attacking by confusing the player with its own bark from 1995.
*   **Tomato Enforcer:** A bipedal tomato with a laser-whisk. It shouts "Yield!" before every attack.
