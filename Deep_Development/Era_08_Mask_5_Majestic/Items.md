# Era 8: Mask 5: Majestic — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Weapons (Tools of Rebellious Heists)

### The Aesthetic Dagger
*   **Description:** A sleek, red-and-black blade that looks incredible in promotional screenshots.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It’s not particularly sharp, but it catches the light perfectly during an 'All-Out Attack.' Style is 90% of the battle."
*   **Effect:** High Crit Rate. Low Base Damage.

### The Overpriced Aesthetic Sword
*   **Description:** A sword that the Trickster worked three weeks at a beef bowl shop to afford. It has 15 different tassels.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It costs 50,000 Gold. It deals the same damage as a lead pipe, but it makes the 'Victory' screen 10% more dramatic."
*   **Effect:** Increases Gold earned in battle.

### The Lead Pipe of Justice
*   **Description:** A rusty piece of plumbing that Suji found behind a gym.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Simple. Effective. Doesn't require a permit or an understanding of trigonometry. It tastes like tetanus."
*   **Effect:** High Physical Damage. Can "Daze" the enemy.

## 2. Armor & Accessories (Protective Persona-Gear)

### Stylish Non-Lens Glasses
*   **Description:** Glasses that are just frames. They provide a sense of "Intellectual Cool."
*   **Flavor Text:** "Wearing these makes you look like you’re calculating a master plan. In reality, you’re just trying to remember if you fed the cat."
*   **Effect:** Increases Luck and reduces the chance of "Confusion" from Math-based attacks.

### The Calling Card (Key Item/Accessory)
*   **Description:** A bright red card with a stylized top-hat logo.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Throwing this at an enemy initiates a 5-minute monologue about their crimes. It also increases the player’s 'Rebellion' meter."
*   **Effect:** Increases Persona damage by 20%.

### Beef Bowl Loyalty Card
*   **Description:** A greasy card with 9 out of 10 stamps.
*   **Flavor Text:** "One more bowl of hydrated beef and you get a free 'Social Stat' boost. The price is your digestive system."
*   **Effect:** Increases all 'Social Stats' by 1 when equipped.

---

## 3. Consumables (Urban Sustenance)

### The Mega-Beef Bowl
*   **Description:** A massive bowl of rice, beef, and questionable sauce.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The fuel of a rebel. It restores HP, but it also inflicts the 'Food Coma' status if eaten outside of combat."
*   **Effect:** Restores 500 HP. Inflicts "Lethargy" (Speed -10%).

### Energy Drink: "Go-2-Sleep"
*   **Description:** A neon-green soda that smells like caffeine and lavender.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The favorite drink of Morgana-ish. It forces the player to skip their next turn but fully restores MP."
*   **Effect:** Fully restores MP. Inflicts "Sleep" for 1 turn.

### Graded Test Paper (F)
*   **Description:** A crumpled paper marked with a massive red 'F.'
*   **Flavor Text:** "Using this in battle shares the player's academic trauma with the enemies. It deals 'Embarrassment' damage."
*   **Effect:** Magic damage to all enemies. Reduces enemy Accuracy.

## 4. Key Items (Heist Documentation)

### The Geometry Midterm
*   **Description:** The source of the Palace's power. It’s covered in illegible scribbles and tear stains.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The ultimate treasure. Stealing this doesn't change your grade, but it makes the Math Teacher cry. Worth it."

### The Fake Student ID
*   **Description:** A card that identifies the Trickster as 'A. Person.'
*   **Flavor Text:** "Grants access to the high school and several high-end cafes that are way too expensive for a teenager."
