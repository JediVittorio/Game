# Era 8: Mask 5: Majestic — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### The Trickster (The Stylish Wild Card)
*   **Aesthetic:** High-contrast, cel-shaded look. His sprite is primarily black, white, and red. He moves with a rhythmic, cat-like grace.
*   **Idle:** He stands with one hand in his pocket, the other adjusting his mask. His trench coat tails flutter in a non-existent wind.
*   **Walking:** A confident, "I’m too cool for this" stride. When he sprints, he leans forward like a Olympic runner.
*   **Attack:** "The Heist Strike." He leaps into the air, the screen turns black with a red "SLASH" effect, and he appears on the other side of the enemy, sheathing his dagger.
*   **Victory:** He adjusts his glasses (which don't have lenses), the screen freezes on a hyper-stylized silhouette, and a massive "LEVEL UP!" in graffiti font appears.

### Suji (The Delinquent Brawler)
*   **Aesthetic:** More grounded but still stylized. He has a lot of "impact" frames.
*   **Idle:** He is constantly punching his open palm and looking for someone to fight.
*   **Walking:** A heavy, aggressive stomp. He looks like he’s trying to break the sidewalk.
*   **Attack:** "The Lead Pipe Special." He swings his pipe in a wide arc. On impact, a comic-book style "CRACK!" appears.
*   **Victory:** He lets out a loud shout, flexes his muscles, and then realizes he dropped his lunch money.

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### Shadow Math Teacher (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A giant, multi-limbed entity made of blackboard chalk and red ink. His "head" is a glowing lightbulb that pulses with "Ideas."
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** He sits at a giant floating desk. He "attacks" by firing heat-seeking protractors.
    *   **Phase 2:** The desk becomes a giant compass that he uses as a pogo-stick to crush the player.
    *   **Phase 3:** He transforms into a non-Euclidean vortex. The player’s sprite is stretched and distorted as they fight the "Void of Logic."

### Aggressive Protractor (Common Enemy)
*   **Aesthetic:** A flat, yellow semi-circle with a single angry eye.
*   **Animations:** It spins rapidly like a saw-blade to attack. When it dies, it snaps in half with a plastic *crunch*.

---

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **Algebraic Demon:** A sprite made of 'X's and 'Y's that constantly changes its value. It explodes if the player doesn't "Solve" it (kill it) within 3 turns.
*   **Parental Expectation:** A faceless, gray man in a suit. He attacks by pointing a long finger and dealing "Guilt" damage (drains MP).
*   **Social Stat Wall:** A literal gray brick wall with a "Knowledge Rank 1" requirement written on it.

---

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Objects:**
    *   **The Grappling Hook:** A glowing red wire that zips across the screen.
    *   **The "All-Out Attack" Background:** A layer that activates during the finisher, featuring scrolling red stars and black silhouettes.
    *   **Cognitive Doors:** These don't open; they shatter like glass when the player interacts with them.
    *   **The Velvet-ish Elevator:** A blue-tinted room that constantly "descends," with a scrolling floor and glowing floor-number displays.
