# Era 3: Last Fantasy Strategies — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### Romza (The Verbose Heretic)
*   **Aesthetic:** Isometric 2D sprite. Sepia-toned palette (Blonde, Brown, Dull Blue). Hand-drawn look with thick outlines.
*   **Idle:** He stands in a heroic "Thinker" pose, one hand on his chin, the other on his sword. A small "..." text bubble occasionally appears.
*   **Walking:** He moves in 8 directions, always snapping to the center of the 1x1 grid tiles.
*   **Attack:** A precise rapier thrust. Before he strikes, a large text box fills the screen with archaic insults.
*   **Victory:** He pulls out a scroll and starts reading a "Speech of Triumph" that continues even after the battle UI closes.

### The Bureaucrat (Administrative Support)
*   **Aesthetic:** Short, slightly hunched figure with a massive scroll-case.
*   **Idle:** He is frantically sharpening a quill with a small knife.
*   **Walking:** A fast, scuttling walk. He leaves a faint trail of ink on the tiles.
*   **Attack:** He throws a bottle of ink at the enemy or stabs them with a giant quill.
*   **Victory:** He frantically stamps a piece of paper and punches the air.

### Agrias (The Overqualified Guard)
*   **Aesthetic:** Tall, imposing figure in gleaming (but sepia) plate armor.
*   **Idle:** She stands at attention, her sword held upright.
*   **Walking:** A heavy, clanking walk that vibrates the screen.
*   **Attack:** "Holy Explosion." She slams her sword down, and the grid tiles around the enemy explode into beams of light.
*   **Victory:** She sheathes her sword and sighs, looking at a pocket watch.

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### Marquis de Merce (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** An elegant, thin figure with a cape made of flowing paper. He is perpetually floating two inches off the ground.
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** He stands behind a floating desk. He attacks by "summoning" Audit Demons.
    *   **Phase 2:** The desk shatters. He uses his cape as a whip, lashing the player with sharpened edges of paper.
    *   **Phase 3:** He transforms into a giant "Zodiac Audit" stone—a crystalline creature with a thousand faces, each one screaming a different tax code.

### The Tax Collector (Common Enemy)
*   **Aesthetic:** A small, imp-like creature wearing a tiny waistcoat and carrying a sack of gold.
*   **Animations:** He moves fast, dodging between tiles. He attacks by stealing 10% of the player's gold and then taunting them.

---

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **Heretical Knight:** A soldier whose armor is slightly mismatched. He attacks with a "Rusty Blade" that inflicts "Tetanus" (DOT).
*   **Audit Demon:** A creature made of swirling ink. It has no physical body, just a pair of glowing eyes.
*   **Zoned Chocobo:** A large, yellow bird wearing a hard hat and a safety vest. It attacks by "pecking" the player in a straight line.

---

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Objects:**
    *   **The Proclamation Board:** Notices flutter in the wind.
    *   **The Grid Lines:** Occasionally pulse with a faint white light to remind the player of their confinement.
    *   **Floating Books:** In the Monastery, massive ledgers float through the air, occasionally crashing into the ground and creating new obstacles.
    *   **Ink Pools:** These bubble and can be "absorbed" by the Bureaucrat to refill his skills.
