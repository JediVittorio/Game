# Era 3: Last Fantasy Strategies — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Weapons (Tools of Litigious Intent)

### The Rapier of Rhetoric
*   **Description:** A thin, elegant blade with a handle shaped like a rolled-up scroll.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Romza’s sword. It deals damage based on how many letters are in the user’s current name."
*   **Effect:** High speed. Damage scales with the length of the attack’s dialogue box.

### The Fountain Pen of Fatalities
*   **Description:** A massive, two-handed quill that drips black ink.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The Bureaucrat’s weapon. One stroke can erase a person’s credit score or their life. Usually both."
*   **Effect:** Magic-based physical damage. Can inflict "Ink-Blindness."

### The Holy Shield of Exemption
*   **Description:** A shield made of white marble, engraved with a thousand tiny exceptions to the law.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Agrias’s shield. It allows her to ignore the 'Trespassing' penalty on Agricultural Grid tiles."
*   **Effect:** High physical defense. Grants immunity to "Fine" and "Debt" status.

## 2. Armor & Accessories (Protective Paperwork)

### The Sepia Tabard
*   **Description:** A worn, brown vest that makes the wearer blend perfectly into the isometric background.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Provides a +10% evasion bonus, but only if the enemy is attacking from a lower elevation."
*   **Effect:** Increases Evasion and Jump stat.

### The Zodiac Audit (Zodiac Stone Satire)
*   **Description:** A small, glowing stone that hums with the sound of a thousand filing cabinets.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It’s not a holy relic; it’s a record of every lunch the player has ever had. Carrying it is exhausting."
*   **Effect:** Increases all stats by 5, but reduces Movement speed by 1.

### The Permit Case
*   **Description:** A leather pouch filled with blank forms.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Reduces the 'Charge Time' (CT) of all magic abilities. Necessary for filing a 'Holy Smite' request."
*   **Effect:** Reduces CT for all skills.

---

## 3. Consumables (Administrative Sustenance)

### Ink-Pot of Recovery
*   **Description:** A small jar of blue ink.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Don't drink it. You use it to refill your 'Permit Case.' Without ink, your words are just noise."
*   **Effect:** Restores 50 MP (used for "Permit" skills).

### Potion of Legal Standing
*   **Description:** A fizzy, bitter liquid in a square bottle.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Instantly cures 'Heretical' status and restores a small amount of HP. It tastes like copper and victory."
*   **Effect:** Cures all status ailments and restores 100 HP.

### The Bribe (Combat Item)
*   **Description:** A small pouch of gold coins marked 'FOR RESEARCH PURPOSES.'
*   **Flavor Text:** "Throw it at an enemy to instantly end the battle. Does not work on bosses, who have higher standards."
*   **Effect:** Ends random encounters. Consumes 500 Gold.

## 4. Key Items (Zoning Documentation)

### The Zoning Permit: Sector 7
*   **Description:** A crumpled piece of parchment that grants access to the Plains of Zeklaus.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Signed in triplicate. It smells like bureaucratic triumph."

### The Deed to Ivalies
*   **Description:** An ancient, golden scroll that proves the entire kingdom belongs to the player's mother.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The ultimate weapon. One look at this and the Marquis will be forced to resign. If you can find a notary."
