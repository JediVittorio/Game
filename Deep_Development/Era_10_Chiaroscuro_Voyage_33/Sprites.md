# Era 10: Chiaroscuro: Voyage 33 — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### The Captain (The Weary Critic)
*   **Aesthetic:** Hauntingly beautiful, high-fidelity 2D. He is lit by a single, dramatic spotlight that changes angle as he moves. His colors are muted (Gray, Gold, Dark Blue).
*   **Idle:** He stands with his shoulders slumped, his massive broadsword resting on the ground. He occasionally sighs, emitting a small puff of "Sorrow-Steam."
*   **Walking:** A heavy, burdened march. He leaves a trail of faint, grayscale footprints that dissolve into pink static.
*   **Attack:** "Masterful Stroke." He swings his broadsword in a wide arc, creating a "painting" effect in the air. The enemy is hit by a wave of thick, gray paint.
*   **Victory:** He sheathes his sword, pulls out his journal, and writes a one-sentence review of the battle while looking into the distance.

### Maelle (The Swift Duelist)
*   **Aesthetic:** Sleek and elegant, with feathered accents. Her sprite is slightly translucent, as if she’s made of a light gradient.
*   **Idle:** She is in a constant, rhythmic bounce, her rapier held in a perfect dueling stance. Her sprite occasionally "jitters" (a frame-skip effect) to represent latency.
*   **Walking:** A fluid, dancing movement. When she changes direction, she performs a quick "Twirl-and-Parry" animation.
*   **Attack:** "Fencer’s Flurry." She unleashes a rapid series of thrusts. Each hit is accompanied by a "CLICK" sound effect, and occasionally a "MISS (LATENCY)" popup appears even if the hit lands.
*   **Victory:** She performs a series of dodge-rolls across the screen, ending in a dramatic bow that is interrupted by a jittery "Return to Idle" animation.

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### The Paintress (Neon Variant) (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A towering, ethereal figure. Her upper body is composed of flowing gray silk and shadow, while her lower body is a chaotic vortex of neon pink paint. She has no face, just a glowing pink eye in the center of her hood.
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** She floats gracefully, "painting" the arena with her giant brush. Her movements are slow and rhythmic.
    *   **Phase 2:** She becomes more frantic. Her silk robes turn into neon pink tentacles that whip at the player.
    *   **Phase 3:** She dissolves into a giant, screen-filling "Splatter." The player must fight individual "Drops" of her essence while the screen flashes in high-contrast patterns.

### Neon Stain (Common Enemy)
*   **Aesthetic:** A literal puddle of bright pink paint with a single, wide eye in the center.
*   **Animations:** It moves by "splashing" across the floor. To attack, it jumps into the air and "flattens" itself against the player’s face.

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **Colorless Soldier:** A gray, statue-like figure with missing limbs and a cracked face. It moves with a stiff, jerky animation and attacks with a heavy stone pike.
*   **Dropped Combo Demon:** A creature made of six arms, each holding a different weapon. It attacks in a "staccato" rhythm, with some hits appearing to "teleport" through the player’s defense.
*   **Lumière Sentinel:** A floating, ornate eye framed by gold ornaments. It casts a beam of light that changes the player's sprite color to pure white.

---

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Objects:**
    *   **The Monolith:** A giant black pillar in the background that "pulses" every time a number is painted on it.
    *   **Pictos (Ink Particles):** Small, black-and-white specks that drift through the air like snow. They turn pink near the dungeon entrance.
    *   **The Gommage Fog:** A thick, gray mist that rolls across the ground, "erasing" the feet of the characters.
    *   **Fresh Paint Puddles:** Animated pink slime tiles that bubble and pop, releasing small puffs of "Neon Gas."
    *   **The Gradient Floor:** Tiles that shift from black to white in a rhythmic wave, affecting the lighting on the sprites standing on them.
