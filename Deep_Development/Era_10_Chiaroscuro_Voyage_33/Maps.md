# Era 10: Chiaroscuro: Voyage 33 — Detailed Map Specifications
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## Overview: The World of Fading Gradients
Era 10 is a hauntingly beautiful landscape inspired by the Belle Époque and surrealist French art. The world is defined by high-contrast lighting (chiaroscuro), dramatic shadows, and a sense of inevitable erasure. However, due to the "Fuzzy Memory" glitch, this solemn atmosphere is being aggressively corrupted by patches of fluorescent neon pink paint. The air smells like oil paint, old stone, and a hint of bubblegum.

---

## 1. The Forgotten Battlefield: The Path of Grief
A vast, misty field littered with the rusted swords and tattered banners of those who were erased by the Gommage. The ground is a desaturated gray, except for the neon pink puddles that look like glitches in reality.

### Map 10.1: The Trench of Artistic Critique
*   **Atmosphere:** Somber and wind-swept. The sound of a lone violin plays a melancholic melody that occasionally skips like a scratched record.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Fallen Giant:** A massive, stone hand reaching out of the ground, now covered in pink graffiti tags.
    *   **The Shadow Anchors:** Points where the light is so thick it slows the player's movement, forcing "Tactical Patience."
*   **NPC Placement:** **The Captain** is staring at a map, trying to figure out why the next coordinate is "Neon 5."

---

## 2. The City of Lumière-ish: The Desaturated Dream
A once-vibrant city of grand arches and cobblestone streets, now reduced to a monochrome ghost town.

### Map 10.2: The Boulevard of Broken Brushes
*   **Atmosphere:** Eerily silent. The buildings are grand but seem to be melting at the edges.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Art Gallery of the Erased:** A building where the paintings are empty canvases. Entering causes the player to temporarily lose their sprite (becoming a silhouette).
    *   **The Pink Fountain:** A fountain that should be pouring clear water but is instead spewing thick, neon pink sludge.
*   **Gameplay:** A "Stealth-Parry" map where the player must parry the "Gaze of the Paintress" (beams of light) to avoid being erased.

---

## 3. The Cathedral of the Gradient: The Sacred Canvas
A massive cathedral where the windows are made of stained-glass gradients. It is the spiritual heart of the expedition.

### Map 10.3: The Nave of the Number
*   **Atmosphere:** Majestic and suffocating. The air is thick with "Pictos" (floating ink particles).
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Gradient Altar:** A platform that shifts through the entire grayscale spectrum. Standing on the "Pure White" tile grants a healing buff.
    *   **The Stained-Glass Glitch:** A window that has shattered into a million neon pink triangles.
*   **Encounter Logic:** Battles with "Gradient Wraiths" and "Lumière Sentinels."

---

## 4. The Dungeon: The Monolith Peak
The ultimate destination. A towering, black spire that pierces the clouds. It is where the Paintress writes the numbers that erase the world.

### Map 10.4: Floor 1 — The Ascent of Aches
*   **Atmosphere:** Frantic. The screen shakes as the Paintress "paints" nearby.
*   **Gimmick:** "The Dropped Input Zone." In this area, there is a 20% chance that a button press won't register, mimicking the user's manual dexterity failures. The player must time their movements between these "latency spikes."

### Map 10.5: The Peak of the Gommage (Boss Arena)
*   **Atmosphere:** A flat, circular platform at the top of the Monolith. The sky is a chaotic swirl of gray clouds and neon pink lightning.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Infinite Canvas:** The floor itself is being painted in real-time. If the player stands on a "Fresh Paint" area, they take damage.
    *   **The Giant Brush:** The Paintress’s weapon, which she slams down to create "shockwaves of color."
*   **Boss Map Logic:** The boss, **The Paintress (Neon Variant)**, paints a number on the player's head. The player must "Parry" the countdown to reset it, or they are instantly erased (Return by Death).

---

## 5. Battle Maps: The Chiaroscuro Arena
*   **Aesthetic:** Extremely high contrast. The characters are lit from a single, dramatic source.
*   **Background:** Hauntingly beautiful sketches of the current environment that are being "erased" by pink ink drops.
*   **Encounters:** This is where the party fights "Colorless Soldiers," "Neon Stains," and "Dropped Combo Demons."
*   **Gimmick:** "The Reactive Parry." Enemies flash a specific color (White or Pink) before attacking. Parrying the correct color grants a "Counter-Stroke."
