# Era 10: Chiaroscuro: Voyage 33 — NPC Profiles
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## The Protagonists (The Fading Expedition)

### 1. The Captain (The Weary Critic)
*   **Visuals:** A tall man in a long, tattered military coat with gold epaulettes. He carries a massive broadsword that looks like a giant palette knife. His face is etched with the lines of someone who has seen too much "bad art."
*   **Personality:** The Captain is burdened by the weight of his expedition’s failure and his own aesthetic sensibilities. He speaks in poetic, slightly pretentious metaphors about "The Gradient of Life" and "The Texture of Sorrow." He is deeply offended by the neon pink corruption, viewing it as a personal insult to his artistic vision.
*   **Narrative Role:** The "Tank" and leader. He provides heavy physical damage and "Aesthetic Buffs" (morale).
*   **Satirical Twist:** His "Ultimate Skill" is "Artistic Critique," where he verbally deconstructs an enemy’s design so thoroughly that they lose their will to exist.

### 2. Maelle (The Swift Duelist)
*   **Visuals:** A young woman in a sleek, feathered dueling outfit. She carries a rapier and moves with the grace of a ballerina. Her sprite occasionally "flickers" or "jitter-rolls" due to the user's dropped inputs.
*   **Personality:** Maelle is determined, swift, and completely ruined by the user’s lack of manual dexterity. She is supposed to be a master of "Fencer’s Flurry," but she often ends up dodge-rolling into walls or parrying thin air. She is aware of this "latency" and expresses constant frustration at her "slow reactions."
*   **Narrative Role:** The primary DPS and "Reactive Defense" specialist. She relies on perfect timing that the player rarely provides.
*   **Satirical Twist:** She has a passive ability called "The Latency Roll," where she randomly executes a dodge-roll backward during emotional dialogue scenes.

## Major NPCs

### 3. The Paintress (Neon Variant) (The Boss)
*   **Description:** A tragic, towering figure draped in shadows and tattered gray silk. She holds a brush that is the size of a tree. Her eyes are glowing pink pits of "Creative Despair."
*   **Role:** The "Final Boss." She is the entity that erases the world by painting its age.
*   **Dialogue:** "I am the hand that erases... but I have run out of gray! Do you like this pink? It’s called 'Existential Bubblegum'! Stand still while I paint your expiration date in fluorescent highlights!"

### 4. Verso-ish (The Mysterious Outsider)
*   **Description:** A figure in a hooded cloak made of shifting ink. His face is a blank white canvas. He speaks in a voice that sounds like wet paint being applied to a wall.
*   **Role:** The guide who understands the "Gommage" and the "Fuzzy Memory" glitch. He provides the player with "Neon-Resistant Varnish."
*   **Dialogue:** "The numbers are wrong, Captain. The Paintress has lost her palette. She is no longer erasing the world; she is... redesigning it. And she has terrible taste in color."

### 5. The Ghost of Gustave
*   **Description:** A translucent blue spirit of a former expedition member. He is covered in neon pink "stains" that he can't wash off.
*   **Role:** A recurring NPC who provides lore about the "First Voyage" and warns the player about "Input Drops."
*   **Dialogue:** "I tried to parry her... but the button didn't click. I felt the pink ink touch my soul... and now I am just a smudge in the margin of history. Watch your timing, traveler."

## Common Enemies (The Artistic Ailments)

*   **Colorless Soldier:** A statue-like soldier that has been partially erased. It attacks with "Static Strikes" (deals physical damage and reduces speed).
*   **Neon Stain:** A puddle of pink paint that has achieved sentience. It attacks by jumping onto the player and inflicting "Visual Overload" (Accuracy -50%).
*   **Dropped Combo Demon:** A multi-armed creature that attacks in rapid sequences. The player must parry every hit in the sequence to avoid a "Dexterity Penalty."
*   **Lumière Sentinel:** A giant, floating eye that casts beams of "High-Contrast Light." Standing in the light increases damage taken.
*   **Gradient Wraith:** A ghost that changes its transparency every turn, making it difficult to target.
