# Era 1: Rolling Americana — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### Less (The Chosen Boy)
*   **Aesthetic:** 8-bit, 4-color palette (Yellow, Blue, Skin Tone, Black).
*   **Idle:** A slight, rhythmic bobbing of the head. His eyes never blink.
*   **Walking:** A stiff, robotic stride. He moves like a toy being dragged by an invisible string.
*   **Attack:** A clumsy, over-the-head swing with his plastic bat. On impact, a small "POW!" text bubble appears and then quickly dissolves into a "SIGH."
*   **Victory:** He holds his bat up with one hand while the other hand searches his pockets for change.

### Carla (The Telekinetic Glutton)
*   **Aesthetic:** Bright pink and red. Her sprite is perpetually floating a few pixels above the ground.
*   **Idle:** She hovers in place, slowly rotating a bag of chips in her hand.
*   **Walking:** She doesn't walk; she glides forward with her arms crossed, looking bored.
*   **Attack:** She points a finger lazily, and a small bolt of "PSI Burrito-Fire" strikes the enemy.
*   **Victory:** She pulls a snack out of thin air and starts eating while the "Victory" text is still on screen.

### Geoff (The Violator of Treaties)
*   **Aesthetic:** Beige and white. His glasses are massive circles that take up 40% of his face.
*   **Idle:** He is constantly tinkering with a small device that occasionally sparks and emits a puff of smoke.
*   **Walking:** A frantic, high-kneed run. He looks like he’s perpetually late for a court hearing.
*   **Attack:** He pulls a massive, impossibly large ray gun out of his backpack and fires a "Legal Liability Beam."
*   **Victory:** He frantically scribbles on a clipboard and hands it to an invisible lawyer.

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### The Lemonade Leader (Mini-Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A small child in a suit that is far too big for him. His hair is slicked back with actual lemon juice.
*   **Animations:** He stands atop a mountain of lemon crates. He attacks by pointing his "Gold-Plated Juicer" at the player, spraying them with high-acidity juice.
*   **Defeat:** He bursts into a pile of loose change and lemons.

### Mr. Adulthood (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A towering, faceless entity composed entirely of gray business suits and neckties. He has no feet, instead terminating in a pile of discarded paperwork.
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** He sits at a massive desk and "attacks" by throwing staplers and ringing a desk bell that causes "Mental Fatigue."
    *   **Phase 2:** He stands up, revealing a body that is a chaotic tangle of telephone wires and calculator keys. His arms are long, reaching across the entire screen.
    *   **Phase 3:** He dissolves into a vortex of beige static. The player must "fight" the static until it settles into a single, mundane alarm clock.

---

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **Stray Thought:** A simple, translucent cloud with a single, weeping eye in the center. It pulsates when it attacks.
*   **The HOA Inspector:** A woman with a beehive haircut and a clipboard that she uses as a shield. She attacks by measuring the player and shaking her head.
*   **Aged Child:** A sprite that looks like Less but with a gray beard and a tiny, pixelated mid-life crisis. He carries a briefcase instead of a bat.
*   **Manic Mailman:** A blue-clad figure whose legs move like a blur. He attacks by "delivering" heavy boxes onto the player’s head.

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Objects:**
    *   **The Telephone:** Occasionally jumps and vibrates when it rings.
    *   **The Lemonade Stand:** Bubbles with a bright yellow liquid that looks slightly radioactive.
    *   **The Fluorescent Lights:** Flicker rhythmically in the Office of Mundanity, casting long, jittery shadows.
