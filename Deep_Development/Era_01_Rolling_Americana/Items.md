# Era 1: Rolling Americana — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Weapons (Tools of Escalation)

### Cracked Plastic Bat
*   **Description:** A hollow yellow bat with a noticeable crack down the center. It makes a satisfying *thwack* sound that sounds like a wet sponge hitting a brick.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It's labeled 'Justice,' but it's mostly used for property damage. It smells like cheap plastic and forgotten summer afternoons."
*   **Effect:** Standard physical damage. Small chance to "Daze" the enemy with the sheer absurdity of the attack.

### The Protractor of Precision
*   **Description:** A standard school protractor, sharpened to a razor edge. Geoff has added a small laser pointer to the side that doesn't actually help with aiming.
*   **Flavor Text:** "For the child who wants to calculate the exact angle of their neighbor's despair before striking."
*   **Effect:** High crit rate. Low base damage.

### Toy Yo-Yo of Entrapment
*   **Description:** A bright red yo-yo with a string made of high-tensile carbon fiber.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A classic toy re-engineered to violate the Geneva Convention. Don't get your finger caught."
*   **Effect:** Multi-hit physical attack. Can "Bind" the enemy.

---

## 2. Armor & Accessories (Protective Neglect)

### Striped Shirt of Maternal Neglect
*   **Description:** A cotton shirt with horizontal yellow and blue stripes. It's slightly too tight across the chest.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Wearing this provides a passive bonus to defense because nothing the world can do to you is worse than your mother forgetting your birthday."
*   **Effect:** Increases Defense and reduces the chance of "Homesick" status.

### Cardboard Box Helmet
*   **Description:** A sturdy appliance box with two unevenly cut eye holes and "HERO" written in crayon on the front.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A masterwork of engineering. If you believe it's a helmet, the monsters might believe it too."
*   **Effect:** Increases Magic Defense but reduces Accuracy.

### The "Cool" Sunglasses
*   **Description:** Cheap plastic shades that are missing one lens.
*   **Flavor Text:** "You can't see anything, but you look like you have a high-yield savings account. That's what really matters."
*   **Effect:** Increases Luck. Grants immunity to the "Judgmental Gaze" status.

---

## 3. Consumables (The Sustenance of Suburbia)

### Damp Towel of Discipline
*   **Description:** A white kitchen towel that is perpetually moist and smells faintly of bleach.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Snap it at a party member to instantly cure 'Confusion' and 'Lethargy.' The stinging sensation lasts for several minutes."
*   **Effect:** Cures all mental status ailments. Deals 1 HP of damage.

### Generic Nutritional Round-Things
*   **Description:** A bowl of small, brown cereal pieces that have no discernible flavor.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Found on the kitchen table every morning. They provide exactly 100% of your daily requirement of disappointment."
*   **Effect:** Restores 50 HP.

### Cold Lemonade (The Forbidden Juice)
*   **Description:** A cup of yellow liquid with way too much pulp. It's suspiciously cold.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Stolen from the Lemonade Leader’s stash. Drinking it feels like consuming a corporate merger."
*   **Effect:** Fully restores MP but has a 10% chance to inflict "Sugar Crash" (Stun) after 3 turns.

---

## 4. Key Items (Plot Advancement)

### Tax Fraud Documentation
*   **Description:** A stack of papers detailing the Arcade’s complex offshore holdings and lemon-based money laundering schemes.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The ultimate weapon in suburban warfare. One look at this and the Town Hall clerk will grant you any permit you want just to make you go away."

### The "Golden" Ticket
*   **Description:** A greasy, neon-green ticket found at the bottom of a ball pit.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Grants access to the Office of Mundanity. It’s sticky. You don't want to know why."
