# Era 9: Boulder's Portal 3 — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Weapons (Tools of Narrative Sandboxes)

### The Gavel of Guilt
*   **Description:** A massive, two-handed hammer that looks like it belongs in a courtroom. It’s engraved with the word "SORRY."
*   **Flavor Text:** "The Oathbreaker’s primary weapon. It deals bonus damage to anyone who has ever stolen a spoon. It smells like municipal metal and regret."
*   **Effect:** Heavy physical damage. 10% chance to force the enemy to "Apologize" (skipping their next turn).

### Daggers: Tragedy & Exposition
*   **Description:** A pair of identical blades that glow with a faint, purple "Edgy" light.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Darkheart’s daggers. One is for killing; the other is for explaining why killing is a metaphor for her father’s lack of approval."
*   **Effect:** Multi-hit physical attack. Successfully hitting an enemy reveals one "Tragic Backstory" point (increasing the party's 'Thirst' meter).

### The Thirsty Bard’s Lute
*   **Description:** A stringed instrument that is perpetually out of tune. It has a sticker that says "PLAY FREEBIRD."
*   **Flavor Text:** "A weapon used to serenade enemies into a state of catatonic boredom. Or to initiate a polycule. It’s a very versatile tool."
*   **Effect:** Magic damage to all enemies. Has a 5% chance to "Charm" the target.

## 2. Armor & Accessories (Protective Romances)

### The D20 of Disappointment
*   **Description:** A twenty-sided die that is heavily weighted toward the number 1.
*   **Flavor Text:** "An accessory that mocks the very concept of luck. Wearing it makes the player feel like they are being watched by a very cruel dungeon master."
*   **Effect:** Increases Crit Damage by 100%, but reduces hit chance by 50%. A "Natural 1" miss deals damage to the player.

### Legally Binding Marriage Certificate
*   **Description:** A golden scroll with a lot of fine print and a holographic seal of a goblin.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The result of a critical failure on a dialogue check. It provides a +10 bonus to Defense because you have 'nothing left to lose.'"
*   **Effect:** Increases Defense. Prevents the player from fleeing from combat (because of the 'Alimony' clause).

### The Aesthetic Eye-Patch
*   **Description:** A leather patch that covers the 'good' eye.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It provides no tactical benefit. In fact, it reduces accuracy. but it makes you look like a character who has 'Seen Some Things.' And that’s what really matters in a dating sim."
*   **Effect:** Reduces Accuracy by 10%. Increases 'Thirst' meter gain by 20%.

## 3. Consumables (Camp Sustenance)

### Artisanal Camp Wine
*   **Description:** A bottle of red wine that costs more than the party’s combined armor. It has notes of black cherry and unrequited love.
*   **Flavor Text:** "One sip and you’ll find everyone in camp significantly more attractive. Two sips and you’ll start reciting poetry."
*   **Effect:** Fully restores MP. Inflicts "Tipsy" (Evasion -10%, Luck +50).

### The "Inspiration" Orchid
*   **Description:** A rare, purple flower that smells like a plot twist.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Give this to Darkheart to unlock 5 minutes of dialogue about her childhood pet. Or eat it to get a 'Reroll' token."
*   **Effect:** Grants one "Reroll" in battle (allows the player to repeat the last action's RNG check).

### Bowl of ISO-Standard Gruel
*   **Description:** A gray, tasteless porridge found at the bottom of a Druid’s pot.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It provides exactly 100% of your daily requirement of 'Generic Fantasy Nutrition.' It’s the only thing that doesn't trigger a romantic cutscene."
*   **Effect:** Restores 100 HP. Cures "Tipsy."

## 4. Key Items (Narrative Documentation)

### The Brain-God’s Pinterest Board
*   **Description:** A collection of mental images showing the boss’s vision for a "Perfect World" (which is mostly just pictures of himself in different hats).
*   **Flavor Text:** "The source of the Absolute’s power. Looking at it for too long causes 'Brain-Worms.'"

### Therespec Mirror Shard
*   **Description:** A fragment of glass that allows the player to see what they would look like if they hadn't chosen 'Paladin.'
*   **Flavor Text:** "A tool for those who suffer from 'Build-Anxiety.' One use and you can change your entire personality. Your debt to the skeleton remains, however."
