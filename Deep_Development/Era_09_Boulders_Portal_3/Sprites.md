# Era 9: Boulder's Portal 3 — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### The Oathbreaker (The Accidental Sinner)
*   **Aesthetic:** High-fidelity 3D-style 2D (prerendered). Massive, dark-iron armor with a matte finish. His movements are heavy and slightly clumsy.
*   **Idle:** He stands with his head bowed, occasionally looking at a silver spoon in his hand and sighing.
*   **Walking:** A slow, thumping walk. The ground vibrates with each step.
*   **Attack:** "Gavel of Guilt." He slams his hammer down with both hands. On impact, a large D20 appears above the enemy, rolls for a "Critical Hit," and then explodes.
*   **Victory:** He kneels and starts reciting a list of his minor municipal crimes while a spotlight shines on him.

### Darkheart (The Cliché Shadow)
*   **Aesthetic:** Sleek, high-contrast leather armor. Her white hair has a faint "magical" glow. Her eyes are perpetually narrowed.
*   **Idle:** She stands with her back to the camera, looking over her shoulder with a "Mysterious" expression.
*   **Walking:** A silent, graceful "thief-walk." She leaves a trail of faint purple shadows behind her.
*   **Attack:** "Tragedy & Exposition." She disappears into a puff of smoke and reappears behind the enemy, striking them with both daggers. A D20 rolls in the background to determine the "Depth" of the tragedy.
*   **Victory:** She sheathes her daggers and looks at the camera. "I have a secret... but I won't tell you yet."

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### The Absolute Heartbreaker (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A giant mind-flayer god with purple skin and red silk robes. He holds a giant D20 that is the size of a person.
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** He sits on a floating throne of hearts and roses. He "attacks" by blowing kisses that manifest as exploding D20s.
    *   **Phase 2:** He stands up and uses his tentacles to "Embrace" the player, which drains MP and inflicts "Romantic Stun."
    *   **Phase 3:** He transforms into a massive, brain-shaped "Lover’s Knot" that tries to "Entangle" the entire party in a polyamorous web.

### Thirsty Mind-Flayer (Common NPC/Enemy)
*   **Aesthetic:** A smaller version of the boss, wearing a tuxedo.
*   **Animations:** He "attacks" by projecting a holographic image of the player and himself at a candlelight dinner.

---

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **Mind-Flayer Simp:** A sprite that is 50% tentacles and 50% "Love-Struck Eyes." It moves by sliding across the floor in a very "thirsty" manner.
*   **Shadow-Cursed Ex:** A translucent blue ghost of a character from Era 1. It attacks by pointing a finger and shouting "YOU NEVER CALLED!"
*   **Thirsty Goblin:** A small, green goblin with a giant lute. He "attacks" by playing a power-ballad that causes the screen to dim and roses to fall from the top of the window.

---

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Objects:**
    *   **The D20 Pedestals:** Stone pillars that glow whenever a roll is about to happen.
    *   **The Magic Mirror:** A tall, ornate mirror that shows a "reflection" of the player with different hair or makeup every 5 seconds.
    *   **Campfire:** A cozy, orange-and-yellow flame that has tiny, floating "Heart" particles rising from it.
    *   **Tent Flaps:** These flutter in the "Golden Hour" breeze.
    *   **Rolling D20 UI:** A large, 3D-looking die that rolls across the center of the screen whenever an action is performed.
