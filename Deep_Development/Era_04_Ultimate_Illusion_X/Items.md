# Era 4: Ultimate Illusion X — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Weapons (Tools of Maritime Debt)

### The Maritime Bond
*   **Description:** A sword that appears to be made of solidified water and broken promises. It ripples when it moves.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Tadpole’s sword. It deals bonus damage to enemies who have a high credit score."
*   **Effect:** Water-elemental physical damage. Increases gold drop rate.

### The Shell of Arbitration
*   **Description:** A staff topped with a spiraling conch shell. If you listen closely, you can hear the sound of a thousand lawyers arguing.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Luna’s primary tool for negotiating with the divine. It smells like sea salt and bureaucracy."
*   **Effect:** Increases Magic Attack. Allows Luna to use "Union Call" skills.

### The Blitzball of Bankruptcy
*   **Description:** A heavy, water-filled ball with the UDE logo on it.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A sports equipment turned weapon. It’s heavy, it’s wet, and it’s legally taxable."
*   **Effect:** Ranged physical attack. Can "Confuse" the target.

## 2. Armor & Accessories (Protective Policies)

### Asymmetrical Sport Jersey
*   **Description:** A neon yellow jersey with one sleeve missing and ten unnecessary zippers.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The pinnacle of Blitzball fashion. It makes you look like a hero and feel like a fashion victim."
*   **Effect:** Increases Agility and Defense.

### Noise-Canceling Earmuffs
*   **Description:** Heavy, industrial-grade muffs.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Absolutely necessary when traveling with Tadpole. Protects the wearer from 'Sonic Laugh' damage."
*   **Effect:** Immunity to "Deafened" and "Sonic Boom" damage.

### The Whale-Scale Ring
*   **Description:** A ring made of a single, massive scale from the Maritime CEO.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It pulses with the rhythm of the ocean and the crushing pressure of a mortgage."
*   **Effect:** Increases Max HP by 20% but doubles the cost of items in shops.

---

## 3. Consumables (Tropical Sustenance)

### Liquid Cynicism
*   **Description:** A small flask of murky, bitter liquid.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Found in Auron-ish’s pockets. One sip and you’ll realize that hope is just a marketing gimmick."
*   **Effect:** Restores 200 MP but inflicts "Silence" (because you have nothing left to say).

### Salty Snack
*   **Description:** A bag of dried seaweed that is 99% salt.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Found on the Besaid beaches. It’s hard to swallow, but it reminds you of why you hate the ocean."
*   **Effect:** Restores 100 HP. Increases Defense for 3 turns.

### Union Hazard Pay Voucher
*   **Description:** A golden piece of paper with a holographic seal.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Can be used in battle to instantly 'Heal' a summoned Aeon. Or thrown at an enemy to make them reconsider their career choices."
*   **Effect:** Fully restores a summoned entity’s HP/MP.

## 4. Key Items (Pilgrimage Documentation)

### The Summoner’s License
*   **Description:** A waterproof ID card with Luna’s picture on it. She looks slightly worried.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Grants legal permission to communicate with eldritch entities. Must be renewed every 500 nautical miles."

### The Spreadsheet of the Ancients
*   **Description:** A massive, stone tablet covered in tiny, carved numbers.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The original blueprint for the Blitzball economy. Reading it causes physical pain and a +5 bonus to Intelligence."
