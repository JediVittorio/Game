# Era 4: Ultimate Illusion X — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### Tadpole (The Sonic Athlete)
*   **Aesthetic:** High-resolution 2D (similar to early 2000s pre-rendered backgrounds). Vibrant yellows and blacks.
*   **Idle:** He bounces on the balls of his feet, occasionally adjusting one of his many belts.
*   **Walking:** A fast, athletic run. He leaves a faint trail of sparkling water droplets behind him.
*   **Attack:** "Sonic Laugh." He throws his head back, and the screen literally shakes. A visible sound-wave ripple emanates from his mouth, dealing damage and knocking back enemies.
*   **Victory:** He performs a backflip into a "forced smile" pose that looks physically painful.

### Luna (The Union Summoner)
*   **Aesthetic:** Delicate, flowing fabrics in shades of blue and white. Her sprite is surrounded by a faint, "magical" mist.
*   **Idle:** She stands with her staff held with both hands, looking toward the horizon with a breathy expression.
*   **Walking:** A graceful, rhythmic walk. She seems to be perpetually performing a slow-motion dance.
*   **Attack:** "The Exit Interview." She twirls her staff, and glowing petals (exit forms) float around the enemy, draining their life force.
*   **Victory:** She performs a "Sending" dance, where the defeated enemy sprites dissolve into glowing butterflies that look like tiny dollar signs.

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### The Maritime CEO (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A massive, screen-filling whale sprite. Its skin is a mosaic of sunken ships, golden coins, and coral.
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** Only the head is visible. It attacks by "Exhaling" clouds of maritime debt (gray smoke).
    *   **Phase 2:** The body emerges. It uses massive, barnacle-encrusted fins to crush the party.
    *   **Phase 3:** The CEO’s "True Form" (a tiny, elderly man in a suit) appears on top of the whale's head, shouting through a megaphone.

### Maritime Insurance Agent (Common NPC/Enemy)
*   **Aesthetic:** A fish-man with a shark’s head, wearing a pinstripe suit and carrying a briefcase.
*   **Animations:** He "attacks" by opening his briefcase and showing the player a long, unrolling contract.

---

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **Underwater Grunt:** A soldier in heavy scuba-armor that looks like a diving bell. He moves slowly and attacks with a harpoon gun.
*   **Jellyfish Litigator:** A glowing, translucent blob with long, stinging tentacles. It pulsates with a rhythmic, "judiciary" light.
*   **Whale-Sized Variance:** A round, blubbery creature that bounces across the screen, dealing physical impact damage.

---

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Objects:**
    *   **The Sending Circle:** A platform that glows with a soft blue light and emits "Spirit Butterflies."
    *   **Floating Water Spheres:** Small, transparent spheres that drift through the air. Occasionally, a tiny Blitzball can be seen inside one.
    *   **Toll Booths:** On the Highroad, these have animated gates that slam shut whenever the player approaches.
    *   **Bubbling Reefs:** The background of battle maps features constant bubbling and shifting schools of neon fish.
