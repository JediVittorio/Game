# Stratum 2: The Hall of False Anchors — Detailed Map Specifications
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## Overview: The Claustrophobic Maw
Stratum 2 shifts from the distorted overworld of Stratum 1 into a series of oppressive stone corridors. The maps are narrow, lit only by small, flickering "Lamps of Regret" that cast long, jittery shadows. The sound of stone grinding against stone echoes constantly.

---

## Floor 6: The Mimic
*   **Atmosphere:** Deceptively peaceful. Smells of lavender and ozone.
*   **Layout:** A single, well-lit room that looks like an Awakening Room replica.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Fake Bed:** Interacting with it causes the room to "bite" (Return by Death).
*   **Gimmick:** On Loop 2, the player must use **Swing** on the walls. If a wall "bleeds" (red pixel particles), it is the entrance to the true staircase. The blood patterns on the wall reveal a numeric code (Meta-Knowledge Var 601).

---

## Floor 7: Weaponized Trust
*   **Atmosphere:** Pitch black, except for the cone of light around the **Smiling Traitor NPC**.
*   **Layout:** A narrow bridge over a spike pit.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Smiling Traitor:** An NPC who offers to guide the player across the dark path.
*   **Gimmick:** Following the NPC leads to a scripted push into the pit (Return by Death). On Loop 2, a new dialogue option unlocks: *(Draw Sword)* "I know what you're going to do." The player must then **Push** the traitor into the pit, using his body as a bridge.

---

## Floor 8: Weeping Angels
*   **Atmosphere:** Intense silence. The air is cold.
*   **Layout:** A large, square room filled with "Guilt Blocks" (statues of weeping figures).
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Guilt Blocks:** These blocks only move when the player’s back is turned.
*   **Gimmick:** The player must backstep across the room. If they face away, the blocks slide toward them, dealing fatal damage on contact. The **Swing** verb can be used to temporarily "Stun" a block and stop its movement.

---

## Floor 9: The Echo Chamber
*   **Atmosphere:** Reverb-heavy. Every footstep echoes three times.
*   **Layout:** A room filled with stone statues that mirror the player's movements.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Echo Statues:** These statues move 1 second after the player does.
*   **Gimmick:** If a statue touches a spiked wall, the player dies. The player must route their movements and **Push** commands to deposit every statue into a "Holding Pen" without ever being followed into a hazard.

---

## Floor 10: Checkpoint 2 — The Awakening Room (Phase 1: Intrusion)
*   **Atmosphere:** The room is beginning to decay. Shadows detach from furniture and move independently. The Stage 1 BGM plays backwards at 50% pitch.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Bed:** Saving now costs 5 **Mind** resource.
    *   **The Puddle:** The Glitch Barista is gone, replaced by a puddle of black fluid in the corner.
*   **Gimmick:** A parallel process checks Variable 701 (DEATH_COUNT). If deaths are high, the room’s geometry begins to flicker, showing flashes of the "Voided State."
