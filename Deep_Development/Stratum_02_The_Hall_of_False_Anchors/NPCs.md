# Stratum 2: The Hall of False Anchors — NPC Profiles
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## The Deceptive Denizens

### 1. The Smiling Traitor (The False Guide)
*   **Visuals:** A man in a simple peasant outfit from Era 1. He has a wide, permanent smile and holds a lantern that casts a small, flickering light.
*   **Personality:** Overly helpful and friendly. He speaks in short, cheerful sentences that feel forced. He is a manifestation of the protagonist's "Betrayed Trust."
*   **Narrative Role:** He leads the player to their death on Loop 1. On Loop 2, he becomes a physical tool (a bridge).
*   **Dialogue:** "It's dangerous to go alone, friend! Let me show you the way to the light. It's just past this ledge... just a little further..."

### 2. The Weeping Figures (Guilt Blocks)
*   **Visuals:** Stone statues of various Stage 1 characters (e.g., Less, Krono, Luna) in poses of profound grief. Their hands cover their eyes, but black ink "tears" leak through their fingers.
*   **Personality:** None. They are predatory manifestations of guilt.
*   **Narrative Role:** They represent the "Watcher" hazard but with a mobility twist. They only move when not being observed.
*   **Satire:** They are literally the heroes the player "saved" in Stage 1, now transformed into heavy obstacles that try to crush them.

### 3. The Mirror Stalker
*   **Visuals:** A shadowy silhouette that looks exactly like the player. It only appears in the Awakening Room (Phase 1) mirrors.
*   **Personality:** Hostile and silent.
*   **Narrative Role:** It reminds the player that they are losing themselves. It does not attack directly but its presence in the mirror drains **Mind**.

## Common Hazards (The Hallway's Grip)

*   **Mimic Wall:** A wall that looks normal but "bleeds" when struck. If the player stands too close for more than 3 seconds, it "swallows" them.
*   **Spike Pit:** A standard RPG hazard, but in this stratum, the spikes are made of sharpened "Zoning Permits" from Era 3.
*   **Lamps of Regret:** These provide the only light in the stratum. If the player stays in the darkness for too long, a "Void-Shadow" spawns.
*   **The Black Fluid:** The remains of the Glitch Barista. It doesn't move, but stepping on it inflicts "Despair" (-30% movement speed).
