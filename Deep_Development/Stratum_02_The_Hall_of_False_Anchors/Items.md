# Stratum 2: The Hall of False Anchors — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Tactical Tools (Contextual Items)

### The Traitor’s Body (Bridge)
*   **Description:** The physical remains of the Smiling Traitor. It is cold, heavy, and surprisingly flat.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The literal weight of your knowledge. You knew what he would do, and you used it to your advantage. It feels like stepping on a part of yourself."
*   **Effect:** Used to bridge the Floor 7 gap. Inflicts +10 **Guilt** resource (Variable 707) upon use.

### The Stun-Bell (Swing Modifier)
*   **Description:** A small, silver bell attached to the hilt of the Rusted Blade.
*   **Flavor Text:** "When you swing, the bell rings with a frequency that paralyzes the 'Weeping Figures.' Silence is their strength; noise is your shield."
*   **Effect:** Causes the **Swing** verb to stop the movement of **Guilt Blocks** for 5 seconds.

---

## 2. Meta-Knowledge Objects (Variables)

### The Bloody Code (Var 601)
*   **Description:** A series of five numbers written in blood on the floor of the Floor 6 Mimic Room.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It’s 4-0-4-0-1. You didn't find this; you bled for it. Remembering it is the only way to open the true path."
*   **Effect:** Allows the player to bypass the "Mimic Trap" on subsequent loops.

### The Traitor's Lantern
*   **Description:** A rusted lantern that emits a flickering, orange light.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It was his only possession. Now it is yours. It doesn't illuminate the future; it only shows you where you’ve already been."
*   **Effect:** Increases the light radius around the player in Stratum 2.

---

## 3. Consumables (Traumatic Sustenance)

### Puddle-Sip
*   **Description:** A handful of the black fluid found in the Awakening Room (Phase 1).
*   **Flavor Text:** "It tastes like cold coffee and a broken promise. It doesn't heal you; it just reminds you that you’re still awake."
*   **Effect:** Restores 10 MP. Inflicts "Despair" for 5 turns.

### Fossilized Rose Petal
*   **Description:** A petal from the Era 1 neighbor’s garden, now turned to stone.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A relic of a simpler time, when your biggest problem was a property line. Carrying it makes the 'Weeping Figures' slightly less aggressive."
*   **Effect:** Reduces the movement speed of **Guilt Blocks** by 20%.
