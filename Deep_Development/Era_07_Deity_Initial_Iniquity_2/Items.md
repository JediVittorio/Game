# Era 7: Deity: Initial Iniquity 2 — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Weapons (Tools of Unstable Chemistry)

### The Mace of Morality
*   **Description:** A massive, golden mace with the word "NO" engraved on the head. It hums with a frequency that is annoying to anyone with a sense of fun.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The Judgmental Knight’s weapon. It deals 2x damage to anyone who is 'Wet' or 'High on Life.' It smells like self-righteousness."
*   **Effect:** Heavy physical damage. 10% chance to "Silence" the target.

### The Burning Log (Staff)
*   **Description:** It’s literally just a piece of wood that Blaze found in a campfire. It refuses to stop burning.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Blaze’s staff. It emits a constant aura of heat that makes nearby NPCs nervous. Don't use it in a library."
*   **Effect:** Increases Fire Damage by 50%. Any successful attack creates a "Fire" surface under the enemy.

### The Cursed Crossbow of Ricochets
*   **Description:** A crossbow made of human bone and bad intentions. The arrows actively seek out red barrels.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A weapon that prioritizes environmental destruction over tactical efficiency. Good luck."
*   **Effect:** Ranged physical damage. Has a 50% chance to trigger any nearby 'Explosive' objects.

## 2. Armor & Accessories (Protective Volatility)

### Cursed Plate Armor
*   **Description:** Shiny plate armor that looks beautiful but feels like it’s full of bees. It glows with a sickly green light.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It provides incredible defense, but also turns any 'Steam' the wearer touches into 'Necrofire Steam.' It’s the ultimate gift for someone you hate."
*   **Effect:** Very high Defense. Permanent "Cursed" status (surfaces react violently to the wearer).

### The "I’m Not a Sourcerer" Hat
*   **Description:** A floppy wizard hat with a sign on the front that says "Standard Hat."
*   **Flavor Text:** "Makes the wearer look so mundane that Magisters will ignore them. Even if they are currently hovering three feet off the ground."
*   **Effect:** Reduces enemy Aggro. Grants immunity to "Searched" status.

### Source-Infused Tea Cup
*   **Description:** A delicate porcelain cup that is perpetually half-full of glowing blue liquid.
*   **Flavor Text:** "For the refined Sourcerer who wants to initiate an apocalypse without spilling their beverage."
*   **Effect:** Increases Source Point regeneration but reduces Max HP by 10%.

## 3. Consumables (Sardonic Sustenance)

### Puddle-in-a-Bottle
*   **Description:** A glass jar filled with 10 gallons of water. It’s physically impossible.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Smash it on the ground to create a 'Wet' surface. Perfect for conducting electricity or just making everyone uncomfortable."
*   **Effect:** Creates a 3x3 Water surface. Inflicts "Wet" on all targets in the area.

### Necro-Firework
*   **Description:** A small, red rocket with a picture of a skull on it.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Does exactly what it says on the tin. Use it to turn a standard fire into a permanent, soul-consuming hellscape."
*   **Effect:** Converts all Fire surfaces in range to Necrofire.

### Cup of "Mundane" Tea
*   **Description:** A cup of Earl Grey that has a 1% chance to initiate a nuclear chain reaction.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Blaze’s favorite. It tastes like bergamot and impending doom."
*   **Effect:** Restores 100 HP. 1% chance to deal 99,999 damage to everyone on the map.
