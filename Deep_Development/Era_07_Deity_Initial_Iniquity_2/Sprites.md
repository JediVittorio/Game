# Era 7: Deity: Initial Iniquity 2 — Sprite & Visual Design
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Protagonist Sprites

### The Judgmental Knight (The Inquisitor)
*   **Aesthetic:** Isometric 2D sprite. Bright, polished white armor with gold trim. His face is hidden by a dark hood.
*   **Idle:** He stands with his arms crossed, his mace resting against his shoulder. He occasionally taps his foot impatiently.
*   **Walking:** A heavy, methodical stomp. He moves as if he’s physically pressing the "Source" out of the ground.
*   **Attack:** "Adjudicate." He slams his mace down. A golden circle appears around the target, and then a bolt of "Moral Lightning" strikes from above.
*   **Victory:** He sheathes his mace and pulls out a massive book of laws, starting to read the "Verdict of the Battle."

### Blaze (The Pyromancer)
*   **Aesthetic:** A skeletal frame wearing tattered, charred robes. His head is a constant flame that changes color (Yellow -> Orange -> Purple) based on his MP level.
*   **Idle:** He is warming his hands on his own head. He occasionally lets out a puff of smoke from his "ears."
*   **Walking:** A frantic, jittery run. He leaves a faint trail of glowing embers on the tiles.
*   **Attack:** "Thermodynamic Sneeze." He lets out a massive sneeze, and a cone of Necrofire sprays from his face.
*   **Victory:** He explodes in a shower of sparks, then reformulates from a pile of ash, giving a friendly wave.

## 2. Antagonist & Enemy Sprites

### The Divine Magistrate (Final Boss)
*   **Aesthetic:** A towering, elegant figure with massive wings made of shifting, prismatic light. He holds a golden gavel that is larger than the player.
*   **Animations:**
    *   **Phase 1:** He floats in the air, "judging" the player with beams of white light.
    *   **Phase 2:** His wings turn into black Necrofire. He descends to the ground and uses his gavel to smash the "Surface" tiles, creating shockwaves.
    *   **Phase 3:** He transforms into a massive "Void-Dragon"—a creature of shadows and ink with glowing blue eyes.

### Magister Karen (Common NPC/Enemy)
*   **Aesthetic:** A woman in heavy plate armor with a distinctive blonde "bob" haircut. She carries a shield and a whistle.
*   **Animations:** She "attacks" by blowing her whistle and summoning "Managerial Reinforcements."

---

## 3. Common Enemy Archetypes

*   **Necro-Chicken:** A small, frantic chicken with glowing green eyes. It moves extremely fast and leaves a trail of green "Necrofire" behind it.
*   **Void-Woken Intern:** A spindly, long-limbed creature made of black goop. It carries a coffee cup and a stack of papers.
*   **Environmental Slime:** A gelatinous blob that changes its color and texture (Water, Oil, Fire, Poison) depending on the surface it last touched.

---

## 4. Environment Sprites
*   **Animated Surfaces (Crucial):**
    *   **Water:** Shimmering blue tiles that ripple.
    *   **Fire:** Flickering orange-and-red flames that occasionally let out sparks.
    *   **Necrofire:** Aggressive, green-and-purple flames that "scream" and have faces in the smoke.
    *   **Oil:** Thick, black, bubbling liquid that slows movement.
    *   **Necrofire Steam:** A thick, green cloud that obscures the tiles below and deals damage every frame.
*   **Interactive Objects:**
    *   **Red Barrels:** Pulsating red barrels that have "EXPLOSIVE" written in 20 different languages.
    *   **Source Vats:** Massive, glowing blue containers that can be "smashed" to release a wave of pure magic.
    *   **The Divine Throne:** A massive, floating chair made of white light that occasionally "pulses" and pushes nearby objects away.
