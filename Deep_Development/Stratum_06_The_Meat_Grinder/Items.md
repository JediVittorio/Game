# Stratum 6: The Meat Grinder — Item Directory
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## 1. Industrial Tools (Interaction Gear)

### The Floating Body (Raft)
*   **Description:** The bloated, distended remains of the Neutral NPC. It smells like iron and rubber.
*   **Flavor Text:** "It’s efficient. It’s fast. It’s an atrocity. You saved 15 minutes of labor by ending a life. The silence of the lake is your only reward."
*   **Effect:** Used on Floor 28 to cross the lake instantly. Inflicts +50 **Guilt**.

### The Bait-Crank (Sound Tool)
*   **Description:** A portable music box that plays the Stage 1 Title Theme at a distorted, high-pitched frequency.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The sound is agonizing to anyone with a soul. To the Sightless Lord, it’s a beacon of false hope. Use it to steer him into the grinder."
*   **Effect:** Used on Floor 30 to lure the **Sightless Lord**.

### Bridge Debris (Pushable)
*   **Description:** Rusted pipes, broken desks, and shattered statues from previous eras.
*   **Flavor Text:** "The literal building blocks of your narrative. You can build a bridge with them, but it will take time you don't have."
*   **Effect:** Used on Floor 28 to build the bridge. Each block placed adds Lore to the **MASTER_LOG**.

## 2. The Lords’ Deficits (Key Items)

### The Heart of Iron (Fake)
*   **Description:** A cold, heavy piece of machinery that looks like a human heart. It does not beat.
*   **Flavor Text:** "You took it from the Heartless Lord’s chest. It provides no warmth, only weight. Holding it makes your **Swing** verb deal 2x damage to constructs."
*   **Effect:** Increases physical damage against mechanical enemies.

### The Sightless Eye (Fake)
*   **Description:** A polished smooth stone that was once the head of a Lord.
*   **Flavor Text:** "A tool for the truly blind. It doesn't help you see; it helps you remain unseen. Holding it increases your stealth radius."
*   **Effect:** Reduces enemy detection range.

---

## 3. Consumables (Slaughterhouse Sustenance)

### Industrial Grease
*   **Description:** A tub of black, foul-smelling sludge used to lubricate the grinder gears.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Eating this is a mistake. Applying it to your boots, however, allows you to slide across the conveyor belts without being moved by them."
*   **Effect:** Provides 1 turn of immunity to **Conveyor** movement.

### Metal Scraps
*   **Description:** Jagged bits of rusted iron.
*   **Flavor Text:** "They provide no nutrition, but chewing on them keeps you awake. It’s a literal 'Pain-Buff.'"
*   **Effect:** Restores 10 MP but deals 5 HP of damage. Inflicts "Berserk."

## 4. Meta-Knowledge Objects (Variables)

### The Sequence (Var 604)
*   **Description:** The exact chain of triggers required to defeat the Moonscorched Gauntlet.
*   **Flavor Text:** "Ego -> Sentinel -> Taskmaster -> Ledger. The order of the fall. You watched it happen in a dozen failed loops. Now, you can execute it in one."
*   **Effect:** Highlights the correct boss to attack during the Floor 29 gauntlet.
