# Stratum 6: The Meat Grinder — NPC Profiles
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## The Grinder’s Victims

### 1. The Suicidal Remnant (The Fragile Memory)
*   **Visuals:** A small, pixelated child sprite with a blank face and a flickering white aura.
*   **Personality:** None. It is a "Death-Seeking Process." It moves toward the nearest hazard with a mechanical inevitability.
*   **Narrative Role:** The focus of Floor 27. It represents the protagonist's desire to end the cycle prematurely.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Silent) . . ."

### 2. The Neutral NPC (The Ferryman)
*   **Visuals:** A figure wearing a heavy rubber apron and a gas mask. He sits on a raft made of human ribs.
*   **Personality:** Indifferent. He doesn't care if the player builds a bridge or kills him. He is simply waiting for the shift to end.
*   **Narrative Role:** Provides the "Moral Choice" on Floor 28.
*   **Dialogue:** "The lake is deep. The labor is long. I have a raft. You have a sword. Do what you were made to do, meat-creature."

## The Lords of the Void

### 3. The Heartless Lord (The Aggressor)
*   **Visuals:** A towering, muscular figure made of rusted iron chains. He has a gaping, empty hole in the center of his chest.
*   **Personality:** Pure, unadulterated aggression. He cannot register compassion or mercy.
*   **Narrative Role:** The physical threat of Floor 30. He must be met with force.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Metalic Screech) . . . NO . . . PULSE . . . NO . . . PEACE . . ."

### 4. The Starving Lord (The Consumer)
*   **Visuals:** A wide, gelatinous entity that looks like a giant, distended stomach. Its "mouth" is the size of the room's entrance.
*   **Personality:** Insatiable. It is a manifestation of the "Lack" the protagonist feels.
*   **Narrative Role:** The environmental threat of Floor 30. It literally eats the map.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Wet Gurgle) . . . MORE . . . STILL . . . EMPTY . . ."

### 5. The Sightless Lord (The Blind)
*   **Visuals:** A slender, multi-armed figure wrapped in tattered bandages. Its head is a smooth, featureless stone.
*   **Personality:** Confused and panicked. It stumbles through the world, lashing out at everything it touches.
*   **Narrative Role:** The stealth threat of Floor 30. It must be guided away from the player.
*   **Dialogue:** "(Whisper) . . . WHERE . . . IS . . . THE . . . LIGHT . . . ?"

## Common Horrors (Slaughterhouse Guards)

*   **Grinder-Dog:** A dog sprite that is 50% chainsaw. It attacks by "Running" through the player.
*   **Meat Hook:** A non-sentient entity that drops from the ceiling to "Snatch" the player, lifting them into the spike-grinder.
*   **Blood-Lust Slime:** A red slime variant that grows in size every time it deals damage.
*   **Slaughterhouse Clerk:** A man in a blood-stained suit who attacks by "Categorizing" the player (inflicting 'Sluggish' status).
