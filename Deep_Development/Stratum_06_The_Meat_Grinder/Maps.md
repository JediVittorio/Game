# Stratum 6: The Meat Grinder — Detailed Map Specifications
**Status:** Deep Development Initial Draft

## Overview: The Industrial Slaughterhouse
Stratum 6 is a rusted, high-velocity nightmare. The aesthetic is "Industrial Slaughterhouse"—massive spinning gears, conveyor belts made of human skin, and meat hooks that dangle from the ceiling. The air smells like hot metal, grease, and copper. The background music is a mechanical, grinding industrial track that mimics the sound of a beating heart.

---

## Floor 26: The Conveyor of Guilt
*   **Atmosphere:** Frantic and mechanical. The ground is constantly moving.
*   **Layout:** A long, linear hallway where the floor is a massive conveyor belt moving toward a spike-grinder.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Spawning Portals:** These periodically drop **Guilt Blocks** onto the belt.
*   **Gimmick:** The player must sprint against the belt while dodging invisible arrow traps (from Stratum 1). Periodically, the player must turn to face the oncoming Guilt Blocks and use **Swing** to "Freeze" them, preventing them from being crushed and adding to the **Guilt** resource.

---

## Floor 27: The Escort Mission From Hell
*   **Atmosphere:** High-anxiety and pathetic. The sound of a child crying is heard in the distance.
*   **Layout:** A room filled with overlapping spike-trap grids.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Suicidal Remnant NPC:** A small, fragile sprite that actively seeks out hazards.
*   **Gimmick:** The player must use **Push** to physically shove the NPC out of the way of traps while fighting minor horrors. If the Remnant dies, the door seals permanently. No **Return by Death** can bring this specific instance back. The player must find the "Alternate Path"—a hidden crawlspace that only reveals itself to those who have failed to save the Remnant.

---

## Floor 28: The Blood Lake
*   **Atmosphere:** Vast and still. The air is humid and smells of iron.
*   **Layout:** A massive chasm filled with a thick, dark-red liquid.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Neutral NPC:** A lone figure sitting on the shore with a boat.
*   **Gimmick:** Two solutions:
    1.  **The Labor Route:** A 15-minute block-push puzzle that builds a bridge using nearby debris. Yields rich lore.
    2.  **The Violence Route:** Strike the NPC and use their floating, distended body as a raft. Yields no lore but takes 10 seconds. Inflicts maximum **Guilt**.

---

## Floor 29: Gauntlet of the Moonscorched
*   **Atmosphere:** Overwhelming and rhythmic. All sounds from Stratum 3 return in a chaotic symphony.
*   **Layout:** A square arena with four circular sub-platforms.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Chain-Links:** Invisible connections between the four boss entities.
*   **Gimmick:** Boss Rush. The **Devouring Ego**, **Sentinel of Regret**, **Taskmaster**, and **Ledger** all appear simultaneously. They cannot be killed individually. The player must manipulate them into triggering each other in a specific sequence (Ego -> Sentinel -> Taskmaster -> Ledger).

---

## Floor 30: Checkpoint 6 — Lords of the Void
*   **Atmosphere:** Absolute void. The Awakening Room is gone. Only a bed floats in the center of a black 15x15 grid.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **The Three Lords:** Simultaneous manifestations of Heartlessness, Starvation, and Blindness.
*   **Gimmick:** The three Lords never coordinate, but their coexistence creates compounding hazards.
    *   **The Heartless Lord:** Attacks without hesitation; must be destroyed with **Swing**.
    *   **The Starving Lord:** Consumes the floor; must be bypassed with **Push**.
    *   **The Sightless Lord:** Stumbles blindly; must be guided into a trap via **Sound**.
*   **Victory:** Defeating all three in a single loop sets the final Anchor before the Descent.
