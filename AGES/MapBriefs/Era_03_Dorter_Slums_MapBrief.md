# Era 3 Map Brief: Dorter Slums
**Baseline Title:** Final Fantasy Tactics (1997)
**STATUS:** IN PROGRESS

## Narrative Premise
The Dorter Slums are a labyrinth of stone and shadow. Here, verticality isn't just an architectural choice; it's a weapon. The "Skeletal Archers" have claimed the high ground, mocking those who crawl in the alleys below.

## Map 022: Dorter Slums
- **Theme:** Multi-leveled stone buildings, narrow alleys.
- **Atmosphere:** Claustrophobic, dangerous, vertically integrated.
- **Visual Style:** Isometric perspective. Gritty stone textures, laundry lines between roofs.
- **Music:** `Battle2` or similar urgent tactical theme.

### Verticality Mechanics
- **Height Markers (Region IDs):**
  - **Region 1:** Height 0 (Alleys/Ground level).
  - **Region 3:** Height 2 (Rooftops).
  - **Region 5:** Height 4 (Towers/Chimneys).
- **Tactical Advantage:** Archers on Region 5 gain massive accuracy and damage bonuses against targets on Region 1.

### Key Events
- **Event: The Archer Ambush:**
  - Multiple **Skeletal Archers** (ID 62) are pre-placed on Region 5 tiles.
  - Battle Trigger: Entering the central alley.
- **Event: Narrative Signpost:**
  - Located at the entrance.
  - Text: "In this era, looking down on someone is a valid tactical strategy."

## Map Brief Summary
Map 022 forces the player to deal with extreme elevation differences. It serves as a combat puzzle where "Climb" and "Jump" stats become as important as "Attack."
