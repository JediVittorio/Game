# Era 2: Temporal Lever — Map Brief

## Objective
Create a series of interconnected maps that represent the same spatial location across different time periods (65M BC and 2300 AD). The core gimmick is "Identical Coordinate Mapping."

## Required Maps

### 1. Prehistoric Jungle (65,000,000 BC)
- **Tileset:** Forest/Jungle (Outside)
- **Atmosphere:** Overgrown, humid, chaotic.
- **Key Feature:** A specific "Bug Event" at coordinate [5, 5] that triggers the timeline shift.
- **Gimmick:** Destructible environment. Using "Bash" on trees reveals hidden items.

### 2. Tomato Utopia (2300 AD)
- **Tileset:** SF Outside (Clean/Red/White)
- **Atmosphere:** Eerily perfect, clinical, advanced.
- **Key Feature:** NPC bipedal tomatoes (use generic RPG Maker monster sprites but set as NPCs).
- **Gimmick:** High-tech lab at [10, 10] where the Fertilizer is stored.

### 3. Ruined Tomato Future (The Boss Arena)
- **Tileset:** SF Outside (Ruined/Dark)
- **Atmosphere:** Blighted, decaying, industrial.
- **Key Feature:** The "Farmer's Shed" at the center of the map.
- **Gimmick:** Boss trigger event.

## Encounter Zones
- **Prehistoric:** Flying reptiles, prehistoric insects.
- **Utopia:** None (Combat is prohibited).
- **Ruined:** Sentient Marinara Golems, Blighted Vine Stalkers.
