# Era 2 Map Brief: The Millennial Fair
**Baseline Title:** Chrono Trigger (1995)
**STATUS:** IN PROGRESS

## Narrative Premise
The year is 1000 AD. The Millennial Fair is a celebration of peace, though the "Seamless Coordinate ATB" glitches are starting to manifest. Enemies no longer wait for a battle screen; they strike where they stand.

## Map 011: Millennial Fair
- **Theme:** Colorful festival, tents, fireworks.
- **Atmosphere:** Joyful, bustling, energetic.
- **Visual Style:** 16-bit SNES style. Vibrant colors, crowd NPCs.
- **Music:** `Town1` (Upbeat, festive).

### Key NPCs
- **Krono-Archetype (Actor 11):** The silent protagonist, currently "practicing" his techniques.
- **Fair Merchant:** "Welcome to the 1000th Year Fair! We've got potions, we've got jerky, and we definitely haven't seen any portals to the end of time."

### Seamless Battle Event: Training Bot Gato
- **Enemy ID:** 35 (Training Bot Gato)
- **Gimmick:** Interactive training battle that occurs directly on the map.
- **Dialogue:** "I am Gato, I have metal joints. If you beat me, you win 15 silver points!"

## Map Brief Summary
Map 011 serves as the tutorial for the Era 2 seamless combat system. It introduces the player to map-based movement and positioning during battle.
