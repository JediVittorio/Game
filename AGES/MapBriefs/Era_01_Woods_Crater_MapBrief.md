# Era 1 Map Brief: The Woods & The Meteor Myth
**Baseline Title:** EarthBound (1994)
**STATUS:** IN PROGRESS

## Map 008: The Deep Woods
- **Theme:** Dark, atmospheric forest.
- **Atmosphere:** Eerie, quiet, overgrown.
- **Visual Style:** Dense tree tiles, dark tinting (handled via Map Event).
- **Music:** `Dungeon1` (Low volume, high reverb).

### Spatial Layout
- **Winding Path:** A linear but twisty path through dense trees.
- **Obstacles:** Overgrown bushes that require interaction to "clear" (simple message text).

### Encounters
- **Troop 6:** Spiteful Snake & Territorial Oak.
- **Encounter Rate:** 1/15 steps (higher density for atmosphere).
- **Special:** Territorial Oak self-destructs at low HP (Skill 523).

## Map 009: The Meteor Crater
- **Theme:** Anti-climax.
- **Atmosphere:** Disappointing, dusty, ironic.
- **Visual Style:** A large pit in the center of a small clearing.
- **Music:** `None` (Wind BGS only).

### Key Events
- **Event: Space-Junk (At bottom of crater):**
  - "It's just a broken satellite dish. No time-traveling beetles here."
  - "The 'Adulthood' anxiety swirl intensifies."
- **Event: Ambush (On exit path):**
  - Triggered when the player tries to leave the map.
  - Text: "Something is wrong. The air is getting thick with... responsibility?"
  - Battle: **Anxiety Swirl** (Troop 7).

## Map Brief Summary
These maps represent the "End of Childhood" arc. The player expects a grand cosmic revelation but finds only trash and internal anxiety.
