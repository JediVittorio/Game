# Era 3 Map Brief: Gariland Rooftops
**Baseline Title:** Final Fantasy Tactics (1997)
**STATUS:** IN PROGRESS

## Narrative Premise
The city of Gariland is under siege. Noble houses squabble over succession while the common folk are caught in the crossfire of "Tactical Grid" logic. The rooftops provide a vantage point for the first major skirmish.

## Map 021: Gariland Rooftops
- **Theme:** Medieval city under siege.
- **Atmosphere:** Tense, strategic, high-stakes.
- **Visual Style:** Isometric perspective (simulated via tileset). Dense urban rooftops.
- **Music:** `Battle1` or similar orchestral march.

### Spatial Layout
- **Elevation Grid:** The map is divided into distinct height levels.
  - **Level 0 (Street):** Region ID 1.
  - **Level 1 (Low Roofs):** Region ID 2.
  - **Level 2 (High Towers):** Region ID 3.
- **Ramps & Stairs:** Visual connections between levels.

### Tactical Mechanics
- **Grid Combat:** Players and enemies move on a 1x1 grid.
- **Elevation Bonus:** Attacking from a higher Region ID increases damage by 1.2x.
- **Obstacles:** Chimneys and crates provide "Half Cover" (visual only in baseline).

## Major NPCs: Romza
- **Role:** The Squire (Protagonist).
- **Dialogue:** "In the grid of life, some are born on the high ground, while others must climb every tile."

## Map Brief Summary
Map 021 is the tutorial for the Era 3 tactical system. It emphasizes positioning and the strategic advantage of height.
