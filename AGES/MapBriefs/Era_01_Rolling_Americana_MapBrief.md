# Era 1 Map Brief: Rolling Americana
**Baseline Title:** EarthBound (1994)
**STATUS:** IN PROGRESS

## Narrative Premise
The town of DirtBound is suffering from a "Synergy Crisis." The local gang, the Arcade Sharks, have pivoted from petty theft to aggressive multi-level marketing and corporate consulting. The arcade, once a haven for youth, is now their "Innovation Hub."

## Map 007: The Arcade
- **Theme:** Neon lights, 16-bit arcade cabinets, sticky floors.
- **Atmosphere:** Vibrant, noisy, nostalgic, slightly grimy.
- **Visual Style:** High-contrast tiles, flashing "Game Over" signs, rows of CRT-glow cabinets.
- **Music:** `SF_Cyber_1` or similar upbeat chiptune.

### Spatial Layout
- **Main Floor:** Rows of arcade cabinets arranged in a grid. Narrow paths between them.
- **Prize Counter:** Located near the entrance. A bored employee stands behind it.
- **VIP Lounge (The Lair):** A slightly elevated area in the back with more "modern" (16-bit) cabinets and a large desk where Frank-Equiv holds court.

### Encounters
- **Arcade Shark (ID 10):** Wandering the main floor.
- **Hula-Hoop Kid (ID 11):** Staying near the prize counter or specific cabinets.
- **Encounter Rate:** 1/20 steps (if using random), but preferably event-touch for Era 1 feel.

### Key NPCs
- **Bored Clerk:** "Tokens are non-refundable. Dreams are also non-refundable."
- **Shark Lackey:** "Have you considered the ROI on a lemonade franchise?"

## Major NPCs: Frank-Equiv
- **Role:** Shark Leader / Corporate Visionary
- **Personality:** Obsessed with corporate jargon, synergy, and market disruption.
- **Dialogue (Pre-battle):** "You're disrupting our corporate synergy! I spent three weeks on that lemonade business plan!"
- **Reward:** Grants Map Item: **Key to the Arcade** (ID 4).

## Map Brief Summary
Map ID 007 is the first "dungeon" of Era 1. It focuses on claustrophobic navigation between cabinets and a climactic confrontation in the back VIP area.
