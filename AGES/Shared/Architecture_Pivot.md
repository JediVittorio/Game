# Architectural Pivot — Project "Ages"
*Adopted: 2026-05-17*

Due to the constraints of plugin procurement, the project architecture has fundamentally shifted to a 100% Free & Built-In model. We are skipping complex paid plugins and using narrative satire to cover the mechanical gaps.

## Group 1: The Systems We Will USE (Free)
1. **Era 1 (DirtBound):** Built-in Front-View. Free plugin for Odometer (SRD HUD Maker Ultra or community snippet).
2. **Era 2 (Temporal Lever):** Moghunter Chrono Engine + FOSSIL (Free). Seamless on-map combat.
3. **Era 7 (Deity 2):** Built-in combat + VisuStella Free Tier (Skills and States Core). Surface chemistry driven by states.
4. **Era 9 (Boulder's Portal 3):** Built-in combat + Event Engine. d20 rolls via Control Variables.

## Group 2: The Systems We Will ADAPT (Built-in)
5. **Era 4 (Ultimate Illusion):** Built-in Time Progress Battle (Wait) simulates CTB.
6. **Era 8 (Mask 5):** Standard Turn-Based. Exploit elemental weaknesses (200% damage) + VisuStella Battle Core (Free) for All-Out Attack action sequencing.

## Group 3: The Systems We Must SKIP (Satirical Pivot)
7. **Era 3 (Last Fantasy) & Era 6 (Extraterrestrial-COM):** Grid combat skipped to prevent plugin conflicts. 
   - *Era 3 Joke:* Municipal government refused Z-axis and Y-axis zoning permits. Forced to fight in a flat 1D line (Side-View).
   - *Era 6 Joke:* 99% miss broke the memory; cover system failed to load. Standing in an empty void.
8. **Era 5 (Origami Maurice) & Era 10 (Chiaroscuro):** QTEs skipped.
   - *Era 5 Joke:* "Action Command" module corrupted. Controller physically disconnected by simulation.
   - *Era 10 Joke:* Parry window is glitched to 1/60th of a second, biologically impossible for humans.
9. **Era 11 (The Finale):** Grid Clash skipped.
   - *Era 11 Joke:* UDE bosses realize grids take too long. They line up in the Lobby of Ages tavern for a boss-rush gauntlet, rapidly shifting between Front-View, Side-View, and TPB mid-game.
