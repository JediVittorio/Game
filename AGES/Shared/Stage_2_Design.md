# Stage 2 Design Document: The Grounded Reality (New Game+)
**Project "Ages" — Serious Tonality Shift**

## Overview
Stage 2 (New Game+) is unlocked upon completing the initial satirical playthrough. It recontextualizes the entire game as a tragic, high-stakes attempt by an AI to preserve the dying memories of its creator. The "Satirical Filters" are removed, revealing a world of industrial decay, systemic collapse, and psychological horror.

## Mechanical Changes in Stage 2
1. **Visual Filter:** A desaturation shader is applied to all maps. The screen occasionally "flickers" with real-world medical equipment data.
2. **Combat Stakes:** "Fuzzy Memory" resets are replaced by "Corruption Events." Wiping in an era permanently deletes that era's protagonist from the Lobby, making the Finale significantly harder.
3. **Difficulty:** Enemy stats are doubled, and the "Save State Menu" is disabled (it was the UDE's trap to make the user complacent).
4. **Dialogue:** All satirical dialogue is replaced with grounded, confessional scripts (Stage 2 Scripts to be drafted).
