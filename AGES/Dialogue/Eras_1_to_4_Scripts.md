# Era 1: DirtBound - Dialogue Scripts

## Scene: The Non-Event
**Trigger:** Opening Cutscene
**Less:** (Yawning) What a perfectly average Tuesday. I thought I heard a meteor, but it seems to have safely burned up in the atmosphere.
**Carla:** (Floats a bag of chips to her hand) Good. The apocalypse is deeply inconvenient to my snacking schedule.
**Geoff:** I've invented a device that bypasses the Geneva Convention! Want to test it on a mailbox?
**Less:** No. Let's just walk around aimlessly until cosmic dread sets in.

## Scene: The Lemonade Stand
**Trigger:** Interacting with the Arcade Delinquent
**Delinquent:** Hello, fine citizens! Care to purchase some refreshing, legally permitted lemonade?
**Less:** Where is the juvenile delinquency? Where is the unprovoked hostility?
**Delinquent:** I have discovered the power of compound interest and small business loans. 
**Choice Tree:**
- **[Buy Lemonade]** -> *Party loses 5G, heals 200 HP.* 
- **[Kick Stand]** -> *Cosmic Anxiety +1.*
  **Less:** (Kicks stand) The narrative demands conflict!
  **Delinquent:** My margins! Why would you do this to a local entrepreneur?!
- **[Audit Taxes]** -> *Cosmic Anxiety +2.*
  **Geoff:** I'm reporting your unpasteurized citrus operation to the IRS.

## Scene: The Nosy Neighbor
**Neighbor:** Have you seen my cat? Also, your lawn is 0.2 inches above the HOA limit.
**Choice Tree:**
- **[Polite Nod]** -> *Nothing happens.*
- **[Call Police]** -> *Cosmic Anxiety +1.*
  **Less:** Hello, 911? My neighbor is exhibiting suspiciously mundane behavior. Send the SWAT team.

## Scene: Adulthood Boss Fight
**Trigger:** Cosmic Anxiety reaches 10.
**Adulthood:** (A swirling vortex of unpaid bills and dental appointments) I AM THE INEVITABLE CRUSH OF RESPONSIBILITY.
**Less:** Finally! A tangible threat we can beat with a baseball bat!

# Era 2: Temporal Lever - Dialogue Scripts

## Scene: The Arthropod Incident (65M BC)
**Ruca:** Krono, watch where you're stepping! That was a *Megaloprehistoric Blattodea*!
**Krono:** (Nods aggressively, draws sword).
**Toad:** Oh, brilliant. You squished a bug. I'm sure the space-time continuum will easily self-correct. It's not like a single insect dictates the evolutionary trajectory of the entire planet. Let's just go to the future.

## Scene: Tomato Utopia (2300 AD)
**Trigger:** Arriving in the future.
**Tomato Citizen:** Greetings, fleshy mammals! Would you like to discuss the socioeconomic benefits of universal healthcare?
**Ruca:** Wait. Where are the post-apocalyptic ruins? Where is the starvation?
**Tomato Citizen:** Oh, we eradicated hunger centuries ago. We are a post-scarcity agricultural society. 
**Toad:** (Sighs heavily) So, because Krono stepped on a bug, humanity was replaced by sentient marinara. And they have a better healthcare plan than my ex-wife's lawyers. 
**Krono:** (Readies sword, looking for something to break).

## Scene: Disgruntled Farmer (Boss)
**Farmer:** Hey! Get out of my premium fertilizer reserves! You temporal tourists are ruining my yield!
**Ruca:** Quick! Use the Fire + Slime Dual Tech to introduce systemic agricultural blight! It's the only way to save the world!
**Farmer:** Save the world?! You're literally introducing a famine!

# Era 3: Last Fantasy Strategies - Dialogue Scripts

## Scene: The Zoning Dispute
**Romza:** Hearken unto me, foul denizens! Thy occupation of this space is a direct violation of the Marquis's holy edict! By the Zodiac Stone, I declare this soil unfit for bloodshed!
**Enemy Lancer:** We just want to attack you! But where is the tactical grid? Why are we standing in a straight line?
**Romza:** The municipal government hath refused our zoning permits for the Z-Axis and the Y-Axis! The simulation literally could not afford the geometry! We are forced to engage in a completely flat, single-file horizontal line!
**Bureaucrat:** Form 8B for 3D spatial dimensions has a Charge Time of 500 ticks. Please take a number.

## Scene: The Wait
**Romza:** (Waiting) ...The church doctrine clearly states...
**Toad (Crossover Banter):** Romza, for the love of all that is holy, just hit him with the sword!
**Romza:** And violate the elevation restrictions?! Heresy!

- **[Offer 10,000G]** -> Rep: Insulting. We strike.
