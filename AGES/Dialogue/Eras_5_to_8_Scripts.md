**Commander Rick:** Alien spotted. Point blank range. 99% hit chance. Take the shot, Rookie.
**Rookie:** (Fires plasma rifle into the ceiling)
**Rookie:** I missed... and the 99% miss chance broke the game's memory! The tactical cover system failed to load!

**Vera:** We're standing in an empty void! There is no cover! I'm just pretending this invisible box is a half-wall!

**Trigger:** Tactical grid dissolves into folding chairs.
