# Era 6: Extraterrestrial-COM 2
**Baseline Title:** XCOM 2 (2016)
**STATUS:** EXPANDED (Stage 2 Initialized)

## Phase 1 (Satire): The 99% Miss
Group therapy because a rookie missed a point-blank shot.

## Stage 2 (Serious): The Failure of Prediction
The "99% Miss" represents the user's real-world motor skill failure. The "Alien Invaders" are white blood cells—the mind perceives the body's internal battle against disease as a high-tech tactical war. The "Empty Void" is the loss of spatial awareness as the user slips into a coma.

# Era 7: Deity 2
**Baseline Title:** DOS2 (2017)
**STATUS:** EXPANDED (Stage 2 Initialized)

## Phase 1 (Satire): Kettle Devastation
A cup of tea vaporizes the continent due to surface chemistry.

## Stage 2 (Serious): The Uncontrollable Chain Reaction
The "Surface Chemistry" is a metaphor for the cocktail of experimental drugs currently keeping the user's physical body alive. The "Necrofire" represents the side effects—agony that spreads through the system the moment a "soothing" tea (medicine) is introduced. **Blaze** is the personification of the user's fever.

# Era 8: Mask 5
**Baseline Title:** Persona 5 (2020)
**STATUS:** EXPANDED (Stage 2 Initialized)

## Phase 1 (Satire): Geometry Exam
Infiltrating a palace that is actually a math midterm.

## Stage 2 (Serious): The Cognitive Decline
The "Geometry Exam" is a literal cognitive diagnostic test being performed by doctors in reality. The "Shadows" are the user's inability to recognize their own family members. **The Trickster** represents the user's desire to remain "stylish" and functional while their memory is being erased by the AGES simulation.
