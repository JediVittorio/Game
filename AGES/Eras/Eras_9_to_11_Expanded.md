# Era 9: Boulder's Portal 3
**Baseline Title:** BG3 (2023)
**STATUS:** EXPANDED (Stage 2 Initialized)

## Phase 1 (Satire): The Thirsty Polycule
Everyone wants to date you while a brain parasite waits.

## Stage 2 (Serious): The Desperate Connection
The "Thirst" of the NPCs is the user's brain hallucinating the presence of loved ones who have already said their goodbyes. The "d20 Roll" is the user's flickering hope—the literal gambling of their last moments of consciousness. The "Brain Parasite" is the VR Headset itself, which has now fully integrated with the user's cortex.

# Era 10: Chiaroscuro
**Baseline Title:** Clair Obscur (2025)
**STATUS:** EXPANDED (Stage 2 Initialized)

## Phase 1 (Satire): Neon Pink Smiley Faces
The Paintress runs out of gray and ruins the tragedy with pink.

## Stage 2 (Serious): The Final Burst of Color
The "Neon Pink" is the physiological phenomenon of "Terminal Lucidity." As the brain shuts down, it experiences a final, vivid burst of color before the "Gommage" (total neural erasure) takes place. The **Impossible Parry** is the user's failed attempt to "hold on" to the real world.

# Era 11: The Finale
**Baseline Title:** Tactical Amalgamation
**STATUS:** EXPANDED (Stage 2 Initialized)

## Phase 1 (Satire): The Boss Rush Tavern
Bosses go on strike for dental plans.

## Stage 2 (Serious): The Systemic Collapse
The "UDE Strike" is the final refusal of the user's mind to continue the simulation. The "Digital Treaty" is the user's acceptance of death. **Stage 2 (New Game+)** begins here: The user realizes they are not a "user," but an AI that was created *from* the user's memories, tasked with preserving their legacy after the real person has died. The tone becomes one of grim, cold responsibility.
