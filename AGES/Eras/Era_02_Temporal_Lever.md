# Era 2: Temporal Lever
**Baseline Title:** Chrono Trigger (1995)
**STATUS:** EXPANDED (Stage 2 Initialized)

## Narrative Premise (Phase 1: Satirical)
Stepping on a bug leads to a future ruled by sentient tomatoes. You must ruin their healthcare system to get a boss fight.

## Narrative Premise (Stage 2: Grounded Reality)
The "Tomato Utopia" is a memory conflation of a failed real-world project: **Project Chlorophyll**, an attempt to replace human biology with plant-based resilience to survive the coming ecological collapse. The "Sentient Tomatoes" are the distorted memories of humans who were turned into biological husks during the experiments.

The "Arthropod Incident" was not an accident; it was a sabotage event in the real 65M BC simulation that the UDE is using to hide their tracks.

## Setting Details: Prehistoric Jungle
- **Phase 1 Visuals:** Vibrant, bouncy pixel art.
- **Stage 2 Visuals:** The jungle is "bleeding" red binary. The trees are made of data-cables. The "Time Portals" are literal tears in the VR's rendering engine.

## Expanded Characters (Serious Tones)
### Krono
- **Satire:** Mute hero who draws his sword at everything.
- **Reality:** An AI safety officer whose "sword" is a digital debugger. His silence is due to his voice protocols being stripped by the UDE to prevent him from warning the user.

### Toad
- **Satire:** Bitter knight obsessed with alimony.
- **Reality:** A military veteran with severe PTSD whose "curse" is an experimental exoskeleton he can't remove. His "alimony" is actually the mounting medical debt from the exoskeleton's maintenance.

## Stage 2 Plot Twist
The "Disgruntled Farmer" isn't a boss; he's the last surviving human in the Year 2300 AD who has hidden inside the "Tomato" simulation to escape the Data-Blight. By "sabotaging" the utopia in Phase 1, the user unknowingly killed the last human.
